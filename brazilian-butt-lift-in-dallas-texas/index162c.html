Brazilian buttock lift in Dallas, TX

Brazilian butt lift is a fairly common plastic surgery in Dallas, TX, the purpose of which is to correct the shape and size of the buttocks and give them more aesthetically attractive and to eliminate the effects of certain injuries.

For the brazilian butt lift used various kinds of buttocks implants, which can effectively solve the problem of satisfying the aesthetic needs of patients.

Features of buttock implants

Buttocks implants in the whole structure is practically no different from other implants (in particular of breast implants).

They represent a strong shell of dense silicone, filled with silicone gel or salt (saline) solution.

The main difference between these implants is the thickness of the shell (up to 1000 micrometers), and as a result – increased strength, as they are subjected to heavy loads regularly.

Fitting Position

Modern implants for the buttocks is usually installed under the gluteus maximus muscle, intramuscularly or under the surface gluteal fascia. This arrangement of the implant significantly reduces (up to 3-4%) the risk of capsular contracture compared with the placement of the implant directly under the skin of the buttocks.

Also placed under the skin of the implant is most often shifted and causes physical and psychological discomfort.

The shape and form of the implant

The shape of the implants can be round, oval (anatomical) and teardrop-shaped. The first and second mainly serve to change the shape of the buttocks, teardrop-shaped – for the removal of aesthetic defects formed after all sorts of injuries.

The surface of the buttock implants can be either smooth or textured. Textured implants are better kept and more durable than smooth.

The choice of form and type of implants for the buttocks is determined by the surgeon in each case strictly individually during the consultation. Properly selected and installed in the buttocks implant does not cause any further discomfort when moving and sitting.

Regardless of the type of implant, you should remember that after the installation of its the injection in the buttocks can not be done, it is possible only in the thigh.

Silicone or saline buttocks implant?

Modern plastic surgery during brazilian butt lift Texas uses, as a rule, silicone implants for the buttocks, due to their advantages:

  • Cohesive silicone gel gives a natural soft feeling buttocks
  • The gel does not flow at break of the shell, only slightly leaking into the surrounding capsule of connective tissue
  • Silicone gel has a good “memory effect”
  • This type of implants, buttocks almost not subject to shrinkage
  • Silicone implants can withstand heavier loads than salt
  • The most significant drawback of the buttocks silicone implants is their high cost in comparison with the salt.

Saline implants have a lot of disadvantages:

  1. They are less durable, inevitably “blown away” with time.
  2. When walking and with loading on the buttocks can be jarring.
  3. There is a high risk of rupture during sudden movement or injury buttocks.
  4. One of the benefits of saline implants – it is their cheapness.

After plastic surgery for buttocks in Dallas, the swelling can bother you, so wear special underwear after surgery, which reduces the risk of possible complications. For the purpose of the best healing, your activity will be limited to about two weeks to remove the postoperative sutures. After four weeks surgery, you fully complete the rehabilitation course.

Texas buttock lift surgery has several features after getting optimum results. Because the weight of your buttocks will increase, to support them, you will continue to wear special shorts for about two months. Approximately three months after plastic surgery buttocks have to be postponed sports and trips to the sauna or steam bath. Also, of course, it is not necessary to increase the weight of buttocks by overeating.

But the ban on the injection in the buttocks area – the only one that will stay with you for life after buttocks plastic. Besides updated and very seductive part of your beautiful body, of course!
