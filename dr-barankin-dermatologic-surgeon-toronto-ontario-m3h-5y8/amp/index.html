Can I get my crows feet treated if I had Botox in my forehead less than 3 months ago?
Treating multiple Botox areas at different times
Nov 17, 2023
There is no issue with treating one area, and then treating another area weeks or months later. There is no evidence whatsoever that this type of practice would lead to Botox resistance. Make sure you're seeing an expert physician injector (e.g. dermatologist) so that you always receive the scientific data-backed truth to your questions.
Is there a reason why one cannot only put 1mls syringe of saline into 100 units of botox?
1ml of saline for 100u botox
Nov 17, 2023
In Canada, most dermatologists actually prefer to put 1ml of saline in a 100u vial of Botox so there is less spread and less swelling (which means less pain). I have been doing so for over 17 years with terrific, precise & painless results.
Can you have Botox to lift brows and smooth forehead at the same time?
Botox for brow lift and horizontal forehead lines
Nov 17, 2023
I would try perhaps 6units (to start) very high up on the forehead, and then place perhaps 4u at the end of each brow for the brow lift. After 2 weeks, you can add a bit more if you'd like. Make sure to have only an expert injector (e.g. dermatologist) treating you to avoid a heavy brow etc.
Do lip fillers fade faster due to electric toothbrush usage?
Lip fillers & electric toothbrush usage
Nov 17, 2023
There is no issue with regular or electric toothbrush usage and the use of hyaluronic acid based fillers in the lips. It will not affect results or longevity.
Female, 41, caucasian - what do you recommend for muscle bulging between eyebrow?
Improving bulge between the eyebrows
Oct 4, 2023
Generally the bulge between the brows relates to overuse & hypertrophy of the procerus muscle and also the corrigator supercili. In most cases, a botulinum toxin like Botox or Dysport works great to weaken & atrophy this muscle so that it is less bulging, and continues to get better & better over time as there is weakening & thinning out of the muscle from prolonged disuse. Your dermatologist can fix this nicely & safely for you.
Can I have Botox with prominent forehead veins?
Botox and prominent veins
Sep 20, 2023
When injecting botulinum toxins like Botox or Dysport, its important to avoid injecting into the obvious veins that you can see or feel/palpate, but also to know the anatomy to avoid injecting deeper veins so as to minimize pain, bruising and other untoward effects. That's why you go to a physician expert injector such as a dermatologist for optimal results, & even more importantly, safety.

Partially dissolving fillers
Sep 20, 2023
I have used hyaluronidase for well over a decade with no problems in partially dissolving fillers, and still maintaining nice and symmetric results.
