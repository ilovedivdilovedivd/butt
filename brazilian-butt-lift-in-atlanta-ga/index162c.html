Types of buttocks lift surgery in Atlanta

The main advantages of plastic surgery brazil buttock augmentation are: the ability to remove excess fat tissue or lack of it to add, eliminate sagging skin, the elimination of congenital defects, and the ability to make more elastic buttocks.

Filamentlifting or tightening by threads

An effective way to tighten the buttocks, and the main thing – to quickly see the results. The procedure itself involves broach special yarns under the muscle of the buttocks, if there is not much slack or excess weight.

But after 3-5 years you should repeat the procedure in order to the buttocks did not lose shape. The filaments injected under the skin via cannula, they are fixed in the tissues by the cone or notches.

Fabrics after brazilian butt thread lift are fixed in the new position, and if necessary – thread lift can be performed together with liposuction and lipofilling. The procedure takes place under local anesthesia. There is no recovery period, as well as scars.

Lipofilling

Buttock augmentation in GA by your own fat – effective method of filling sagging buttocks. In other words, this procedure may be called buttock augmentation with implants. This correction is only possible if the patient has a body fat. This fat is collected, treated in a special way, and then injected into the desired area. The effect persists for a long time, if there is no a significant jump of body weight. The procedure lasts approximately 30 minutes or an hour under local anesthesia, rehabilitation is not required.

Contraindications for brazilian butt lift

As a rule, there are contraindications for almost all surgical procedures. They also exist for buttock augmentation with fat transfer. Implementation of the intervention is contraindicated in the presence of chronic diseases such as diabetes, cancer, heart and vascular pathology, etc. Doctors do not recommend to do surgery if you have violation of blood coagulation functions.

In addition to all the above surgery is contraindicated in the presence of chronic diseases in the acute stage. Of course, much more contraindications to plastic surgery of buttocks than to the non-surgical thread lift. These include:

  1. excess weight
  2. intolerance to anesthesia, a weak heart;
  3. pregnancy and lactation;
  4. tendency to form keloid scars;
  5. strand implants under the skin, inserted earlier;
  6. temperature, skin inflammation, skin diseases;
  7. obesity.
