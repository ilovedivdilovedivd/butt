Mid thirties corner of mouth skin fold, what are my options?
No one answer
Jul 2, 2017
There is no one answer for this. Whatever you choose, I would suggest a treatment which is going to give you lift to reduce the heaviness at the mouth corners rather than directly injecting them. You could try thread lifting, Ultracel, Ultherapy, facelift using dermal fillers. I find a combination approach works best so a little bit of each.
Is it ok to work out the day after having Biorevitalization?
Why not?
Jul 2, 2017
The only reason you would not exercise is if there is bruising as putting up your blood pressure during exercise can make the bruising worse. I ask my patients not to exercise for a couple of days after the procedure as sometimes there can be a small nick in a vessel which hasn’t yet presented as a bruise, but an increase in blood pressure or a plane ride can make one appear, so please be aware of this.

Watch out for vessel occlusion
Jul 2, 2017
Thats a nasty looking bruise you have. It is not uncommon to bruise from lip injections, but one thing you have to watch out for is occlusion of a blood vessel with the filler after the treatment is done. This can sometimes present like a bruise after treatment, but the area very quickly goes almost black in appearance, not quite like a normal bruise. Reinjected after lip filler Infection?
Sure it was all gone?
Jul 2, 2017
I wold ask if you are sure that the infection and original filler was completely gone after the first round. Some products are harder to break down than others and often require multiple sessions with Hyalase, perhaps you had a small amount left in situ with a biofilm around it. I would also ask whether you had systemic antibiotics too because this might have helped if you had residual infection.
What can be done about the unevenness in my lips ?
More complicated than it appears
Jul 15, 2016
Hi, the reason your lip is lower on one side is likely due to the cheek not having enough volume on the side. If there is not enough volume the muscle which elevates the I cannot work properly and it appears to drop. You should augment the cheek on your right and then worry about the lip. It’s not caused by the mole being there. I would suggest hyaluronic acid rather than fat because there is a greater level of control achievable.
