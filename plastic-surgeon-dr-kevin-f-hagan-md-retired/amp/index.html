Mar 22, 2011

Columellar Strut – When is It Necessary?
Maintaining nasal tip position
Mar 22, 2011
A columellar strut is just one method of maintaining or increasing nasal tip projection. Other techniques such as tip grafting and various suture techniques are also effective, and each surgeon has his or her favorite methods. Following rhinoplasty, the nasal tip almost always drops a bit no matter what technique is used.  The question is, do you feel that your tip is now too low?  If so, discuss this with your surgeon.
