Are there any unwanted side effects from an internal mesh? Does it really work?
Internal mesh/scaffold
May 24, 2022
An internal mesh, such as GalaFLEX, is a bioresorbable scaffold that supports, reinforces, and elevates soft tissues. The mesh completely absorbs after 1.5 to 2 years leaving the tissue 2-4 times stronger than before. From my point of view and according to many studies, the mesh seems to really work.

Breast share from shallow to projection
May 24, 2022
In my opinion, the only way to change the breast shape from shallow to projected is with implants. It’s not recommend to use hyaluronic acid and unfortunately with fat grafting, it’s very difficult to achieve a good result in regards to projection.
Breast augmentation with lift – does the areola get darker?
Areola darkening after breast augmentation with lift
May 24, 2022
After undergoing a breast augmentation with lift, a patient may experience darkening of the areola. Overall, they will remain the same color but they can become darker or lighter depending on any complications that may occur related to the areola. For example, patients that are frequent smokers have a higher risk of experiencing areola complications.

Can I wear a bikini 1 year after tummy tuck?
Sep 27, 2021
