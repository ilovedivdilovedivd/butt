How can I be sure the Fat Transfer that reabsorbs will do so in an even and symmetrical way?
Facial fat transfer
Oct 13, 2015
Fat transfer to the face is an alternative to fillers, when considering adding volume in areas such as the cheeks, cheekbones, tear trough, lips. It is not as precise a method as HA fillers and has a rather unpredictable result. However, when injected symmetrically in volume, depth and technique, it is really very uncommon to notice any assymetry after the absorbtion. In order to minimize this risk, multiple imjections are more preferable, one every 4 weeks.
