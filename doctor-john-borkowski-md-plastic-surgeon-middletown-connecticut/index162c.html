Why are edges so bumpy and uneven after labiaplasty and will it change?

May 23, 2014
Early distortion of the labia is common after all methods of labia reduction. The dissolving sutures hold the tissue tight and the swelling around the stitches causes the bumps that you see. As noted by the other surgeons, you will see the result in about 3 months, but additional softening of the labia can take up to 6 months.
