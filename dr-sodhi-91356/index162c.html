Photorejuvenation and pregnancy: what is the risk of having treatment while pregnant?
Risks of Photorejuvenation during pregnancy
Feb 11, 2019
While photorejuvenation likely poses no risk to your baby, your hormone levels shift dramatically during pregnancy and could predispose you to hyperpigmentation and worsening of melasma. I am an OB and recommend that all of my pregnant patients avoid such services until 6 weeks postpartum.
