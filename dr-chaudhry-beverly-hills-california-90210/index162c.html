Can I choose to have rhinoplasty done in a hospital instead of an office for safety reasons?
Office OR
Nov 30, 2019
Hi
As long as the OR is accredited by a legitimate governing body (i.e. AAAASF, AAHC, JACHO etc)., performing surgery in the office OR is safe. I would even argue infection risks are lower too as hospital OR’s are also used for types of patients, even those with active infections. You could use a hospital OR, but the cost will be significantly higher.

Best,
