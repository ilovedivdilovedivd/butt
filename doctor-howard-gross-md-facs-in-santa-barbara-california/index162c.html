What is the best way to correct capsular contracture? I have a grade 4 where my implant is hard
Make sure to improve situation
Dec 23, 2015
With a grade 4 capsule, total capsulectomy is usually required. Once that is done, it is important to try to improve your likelihood of a good result going forward. If your implant is not under the muscle, the new one should be placed submuscular. If the implants are smooth, consider textured. If the implants are old, new ones are best. Finally, you may consider saline implants which may encapsulate less in some people.

If you gain weight after Liposuction, will it go to different areas or those same areas? I'm 4 w
wherever the fat cells are
Dec 10, 2015
The goal of liposuction is to re-contour your body.The fat cells remaining, both in the area that was treated and other areas, are now in a better proportion to each other. Weight gain (or loss for that matter) will be from all areas in proportion to where the remaining fat cells are. You will not get back the problem areas that were suctioned.
