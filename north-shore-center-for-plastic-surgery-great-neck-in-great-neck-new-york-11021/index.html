Can I get a filler (such as Juvederm Voluma) if I had PMMA injected in my face in the past?
You can get any HA filler after PMMA injection in the past!
Nov 11, 2019
Great Question! Yes, there is no contraindication to getting any HA filler (such as Voluma) after receiving a PMMA filler (specially so long ago). With that said, Voluma is not my 1st choice (or second or third) for marionette lines – You want to try a filler that is injected more superficially for those lines such as Juvederm Ultra, Ultra plus or vollure. Hope this helps!

There is a small change of an allergic reaction to Juvederm, but it is not very common. Juvederm is made out of Hyalouronic acid which already exist in your body so patients usually tolerated well. Now Juvederm usually comes mixed in with Lidocaine for patient comfort which some patients might be allergic too. Make sure to discuss with your doctor prior to injections. How long will lip fillers last with 2 syringes of Juvederm filler?
Lip fillers
Mar 27, 2019
Good Morning, a lot of what you are seeing right now is swelling from the injection itself. You should give it at least two weeks for it all to settle. It should last a little longer since you got two syringes, but that varies depending on the type of filler and the person (some people metabolize the HA faster).
