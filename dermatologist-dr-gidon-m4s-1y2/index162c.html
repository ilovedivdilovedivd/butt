When will my skin smooth out after Fraxel Dual?
Fraxel Dual laser
Nov 25, 2023
The Fraxel Dual is an effective treatment for stimulating collagen, reducing sun damage and brightening the complexion. Once the sandpapery texture flakes off, there can be some redness and it can take a few weeks to see the result. If someone is prone to acne, there can be a flare which a dermatologist can treat.
Could Botox fix my uneven/asymmetrical jaw?
Chin Botox
Nov 25, 2023
Botox injected into the chin muscles can help relax the muscle and smooth the chin. Filler can also be added to create more symmetry. Please consult an experienced injector for assessment and treatment.
Masseter Botox and Jowls/Marionette Lines
Botox for masseters
Nov 25, 2023
Botox for the masseters can help with grinding and clenching of the teeth and can also slim the face. A small amount can be used initially and then increased if necessary. In a young individual, there would probably not be a change in the jowls and the effect does wear off if you don’t like the result. A downturned mouth can also be treated.
Female, 41, caucasian – what do you recommend for muscle bulging between eyebrow?
Bulging glabellar muscle
Nov 25, 2023
Neuromodulators can relax the glabellar muscles that cause the bulge. Please consult an experienced injector who can assess the muscle movement and add more neuromodulator.
Can I get my lips dissolved and jawline filler on the same day?
Dissolving lip filler
Nov 25, 2023
Since treatment for the lips and jawline are two distinct areas, it would be possible to have filler in your lips dissolved and filler injected to enhance the jawline on the same day.
Would like to continue Xeomin regime after brain stent surgery & ongoing daily baby aspir
Xeomin and Aspirin
Jul 21, 2023
Neuromodulator injections, such as Xeomin, can be done while on Aspirin but there is an increased chance of bruising. Using an arnica cream at the injection sites for a few days before and after the injections can minimize bruising and makeup can also be applied. Avoidance of green tea, alcohol, ibuprofen, vitamin E and omega 3s several days before and 24 hours after injections can also minimize bruising.
Dermal filler for asymmetry?
Fillers for the jaw and chin
Jul 21, 2023
Strategically placed fillers can help lift jawline sagging, provide symmetry and improve the projection and contour of the chin with some potential bruising but otherwise, minimal downtime. Please consult a cosmetic dermatologist for an assessment.
