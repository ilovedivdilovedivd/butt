Is 8 weeks too much time off from work for a breast reduction?
Time off work for a breast reduction
Aug 15, 2018
Most patients need at least 2 weeks off for a breast reduction.  Nurses at the hospital I work at are given 2 weeks by their employer, the hospital, and they do well.  They have to limit lifting and try to ask for extra help if needed until they feel strong again, which is typically 4 weeks.  For more physical jobs, like police work, I do ask patients to hold off for at least a month, maybe 6 weeks.  Best of luck.  
Will the fat stay in your hip area after getting a BBL?
Fat transfer for hip dips
Jul 31, 2018
Nobody likes hip dips.  Fat transfer with a BBL can correct this when done properly.  The combination of removal of fat above, and sometimes below, the dip with filling into the dip can often fully correct the issue.  Be sure to consult with a plastic surgeon board-certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery.  Best of luck.
Sad about tummy tuck results: Is there a solution?
Revision abdominoplasty is possible
May 4, 2018
The scar is high.  That's unfortunate.  A revision abdominoplasty is possible.  In this procedure the incision is just made lower and the tissue is re-elevated off of the muscles and pulled down tight.  This is the same as a mini-tummy tuck.  Enough time has to pass for some laxity to develop in the lower abdominal skin.  On the sides, the scars can be revised as well.  An in-person consultation is always needed to determine if the procedure is right for you.
