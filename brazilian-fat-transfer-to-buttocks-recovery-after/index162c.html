Brazilian Butt Lift Recovery

Brazilian Butt Lift is a revolutionary procedure to not only lift the butt but also to make the butt fuller and rounder with a better overall shape. In this procedure we lipsouction fat from other areas of the body and then transplant that fat into the buttock.

The operation is performed under a very light anesthetic. The patient is home in a few hours and usually back to work in a few days to a week. The beauty of this operation is that no implants need to be used. Also you will get the added advantage of reduction of unwanted fat in other areas of your body. Our patients are usually say the procedure was much easier than they thought it was going to be and are delighted with their new look. (Frank Lista, MD, Toronto Plastic Surgeon)

Most patients take pain medicine for 2-4 days, and gradually increase their activities until returning to work at 1 week. I require patients to begin walking, preferably outside, the day after surgery. Some patients can start working out a bit the 3rd week after surgery. The final go-ahead for exercise and pressure on the buttock comes at 4 weeks in my practice. Usually the recovery time are between 10-14 days after swollen of liposuction go down.
