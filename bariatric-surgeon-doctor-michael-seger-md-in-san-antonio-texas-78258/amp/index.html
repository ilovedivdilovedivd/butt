It’s been 5 months since my gastric bypass. Should I get body lift surgery or wait a full year?
Plastic surgery after gastric bypass
Feb 25, 2015
After weight loss surgery, it’s usually recommended to wait at least one year to 18 months or when you’re at goal weight before having plastic surgery. If you get the plastic surgery too soon after bariatric surgery and you are not at your goal weight, then you’ll still have excess skin and possibly more skin complications later.
