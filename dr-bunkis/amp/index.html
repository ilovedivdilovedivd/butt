Would a brachio be best with a skin tightening treatment for my excess wrinkly skin on forearm?
Lasers won’t help
Sep 26, 2017
A laser won’t significantly tighten arm skin. The skin there is thin and a more vigorous laser treatment is likely to depigment the skin and cause scaring. You would be much better off just having the excess skin excised – at the price of a scar along your inner arm to as far as you want the skin removed.
I have wrinkles and sagging from corners of mouth to chin, making me look OLD. How can I fix thi
Everyone ages at a different speed
Sep 26, 2017
You are showing early signs of sagging of the muscles under your skin that happens to everyone eventually, but yes, you are getting it earlier in life than the average person. When it bothers you enough, I would have a facelift and have the muscles lifted to where they were when you were 20 years old. As the muscles droop, you get laxity and fullness lateral to the mouth, in the jowl area and under the chin, making the face look pear shaped – a youthful face looks V shaped…
Can sitting for long periods of time after a BBL cause problems?
Nothing to do with sitting.
Sep 26, 2017
When fat is moved from one part of your body to another, much of it will not make the transfer and will die. Rarely will you see persistent results one or two years after a Brazilian Butt Lift. If you want predictable and long lasting results, consider a buttocks implant procedure.

Question about sutures after otoplasty.
Go see your surgeon!
Sep 12, 2017
If you are feeling and seeing sutures, go see your surgeon and have them removed. Sutures are necessary initially to help keep the cartilage in the desired position, but in a few months, the cartilage is healed and the sutures are not required any longer. If they remain buried and are not an issue, no need to remove them, but if they are protruding or palpable, just go in and have them removed.
