It is worth noting that men are often solved to such surgery. Buttocks augmentation surgery can be done at almost any age, and the main condition – the state of health at the time of plastic surgery on your buttocks.

Carrying out of brazilian butt lift surgery

San Diego surgery is performed under general anesthesia within from a half to two hours. In the crease, in the sacrum between the buttocks is made an incision, peel off the tissue and under the gluteus maximus created a kind of “pockets”. In those “pockets” implant is installed. It does not require a special fixation, on the contrary – it should move freely in their “pocket”.

After surgery on the tissue overlap surgical sutures, on the skin – cosmetic. They are removed on the 12th day after surgery. Scars disappear quickly enough, clear traces are left in rare circumstances.

Complications

Complications are possible after brazilian lift buttocks. They are specific to any surgeries: the feeling of pain, fever, a temporary drop in sensitivity. During the first week it is not recommended to sit on the buttocks.

For two months is advisable to wear a special compression underwear that is tight-fitting buttocks. After 6 weeks is allowed exercise. In general, complications are rare and often associated with inflammatory processes in the surgical wound, bleeding at the site of incision or scar changes.

Prohibition of intramuscular injections in the buttocks will be accompanied for all life a patient who was lift the buttocks – injections can damage the implant.
