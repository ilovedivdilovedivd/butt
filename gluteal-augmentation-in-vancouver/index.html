After the operation, there is nothing more significant than improperly installed gluteal implants, which can look very strange for some people.

In cosmetic surgery, professional skill of most surgeons quite noticeable on plastic surgeries that they perform. It is important to understand that the aesthetic style of the surgeon and his ability to convey the beauty comes with age, when he performs certain surgeries regularly.

3. And, perhaps most importantly, you need to take into account in the process of your choice – the chance of developing an infection.

Skin incision, when performing buttock augmentation with implants, placed in such part of the body where there is an increased risk of bacterial infection. Of course, it does not depend on you, because the operating incision is close to the rectal area, from where severe infection may spread.

This aspect of the surgical operation can not be underestimated, as some little thing that can not happen. If your chosen surgeons say, there is no problem – is necessary to put his qualifications questioned.

Fact: if the Vancouver’s surgeon does not perform buttock augmentation regularly – you are at increased risk of severe infection. In some cases, the development of infection requires the removal of the implant in a matter of urgency that will cause you a lot of pain for a long time.
