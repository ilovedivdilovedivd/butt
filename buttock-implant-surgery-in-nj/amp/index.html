Buttock implant surgery in New Jersey

With their help, the surgeon can improve the shape of the buttocks, if your buttocks dropped and became flatter due to sedentary lifestyle or age.

There are cases when a woman is necessary to restore the symmetry of the buttocks – they can be deformed as a result of injury or struck disease. In this case, the buttocks lift is a real salvation.

During buttocks plastic, silicone implant is placed in the cavity between the buttocks muscle or under the fatty tissue, usually in the upper part of the buttocks.

Incisions can be made in different places – in the fold under the buttocks, between the buttocks, which is a real salvation. Scarring after plastics buttocks are inevitable, so it is very important that they are well healed and become inconspicuous.

To do this, the doctor will prescribe you at least a few weeks before the buttocks enhancement, stop smoking and taking birth control pills.

Preparation for brazilian butlift – a standard procedure adopted for most plastic surgeries. Surgery itself lasts one and a half to two hours and is conducted mainly under general anesthesia.

During surgery, the surgeon makes a small incision in the gluteal crease between, through which installs implants under the gluteus maximus. The scar from the incision heals quickly and becomes barely noticeable, moreover, it hides by natural folds of the body.

Anesthesia: local or general.

Duration of stay in the hospital: 2 days or 10 days to be sure in good wound healing

Postoperative recovery

Small discomfort will be a few days, then – almost none. Full recovery will occur after 4 weeks. One and a half months after buttocks lift surgery have to wear a special compression garment, it is not recommended to jump in and do some exercise, you can not bathe in the sauna and bath.

Strict prohibition – injection. Injections can be done only in the region of the hip for all life after the establishment of the gluteal prosthesis.

Despite the seeming simplicity of buttock implants, plastic surgery requires sufficient experience from the a plastic surgeon, accuracy and refined technique, as he faces a challenge to get around the sciatic nerve and other important nerves and blood vessels.

Medical error may lead to violations of motor activity. The patients spend in the hospital a day after surgery, and then sent home with the following recommendations:

  • Personal hygiene,
  • Wearing a special compression underwear that helps to fix the implant in position,
  • Avoiding alcohol and smoking at the time of recovery,
  • Limitation of physical activity, including a limit on the seating on the buttocks.

After a couple of weeks after the buttocks augmentation in New Jersey the patients return to normal life, but the serious load limit is stored up to 2-3 months after surgery. During this same time, it is recommended to wear supportive underwear. The only inconvenience that may cause you gluteal implants – is the impossibility of intramuscular injections in this area. In the event of the need for treatment by such methods, you have to do injections in the thigh.

The first time the buttocks may look swollen, possibly feeling light tingling or numbness. All these effects disappear two to three weeks after surgery. In the postoperative period recommended day and night wearing compression underwear – shorts, tightly covering buttocks. This prevents displacement of the implant and buttocks deformation.

For sports activities you can return after a consultation with the surgeon in a month after the surgery. The final result after Brazilian butt lift in New Jersey can be assessed six months after surgery.
