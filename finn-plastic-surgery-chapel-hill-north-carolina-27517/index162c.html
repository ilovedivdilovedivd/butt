Saddle nose. Is there a non surgical procedure that will help the appearance?
Yes
Oct 23, 2020
Yes repair of a saddle nose is challenging, but possible. This usually requires using one of your own ribs for repair. If your nose is fairly solid, you may get some improvement with just an injectible filler for a temporary benefit. Good luck!
What is the best xanthelasma treatment currently available?
surgical excision
Aug 17, 2020
Surgery is the best solution for xanthelasma. This can usually be done under local anesthesia in the office. Eyelid skin heals extremely well. Unfortunately, this condition can recur over several years. If it does, excision can be repeated.
