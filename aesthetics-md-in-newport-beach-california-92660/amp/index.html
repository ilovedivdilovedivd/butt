Doctors

Mark Anton, MD, FACS

Name: Mark Anton, MD, FACS
Statement: Dr. Mark Anton is a board-certified plastic surgeon and the founder of Anton Aesthetics in Newport Beach, serving the greater Los Angeles area.

He is particularly well-known for his exceptionally natural-looking results from breast enhancements, tummy tucks, liposuction, and complete mommy makeovers, as well facelifts and rhinoplasty. His patients can also choose from a range of nonsurgical treatment options, including Botox, dermal fillers, IPL photofacials, microneedling, and SculpSure.

“After more than 31 years as a plastic surgeon, I've learned that how we feel and think about ourselves is reflected in our self-confidence,” says Dr. Anton. “A good plastic surgeon is like a sculptor who enhances each patient’s natural beauty, without losing the integrity of its individuality.”

Dr. Anton obtained his undergraduate degree in biology at Tufts University before earning his medical degree at Wake Forest University Bowman Gray School of Medicine in Winston-Salem, North Carolina.

Following medical school, Dr. Anton honed his surgical technique with a robust series of residencies and fellowships. These include a residency in General Surgery and residency in Plastic Surgery, both at William Beaumont Hospital in Royal Oak, Michigan. He also completed two fellowships: one in Facial Plastic Surgery at Eastern Virginia Medical School in Norfolk, and a second in Breast Plastic Surgery at St. Joseph Hospital in Atlanta, Georgia.

Dr. Anton is board-certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery and a member of the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery (ASAPS), American Society of Plastic Surgeons (ASPS), and is a fellow of the American College of Surgeons (FACS).

In addition to his impressive medical education, Dr. Anton has received numerous awards and recognition for excellence in his field. He has been named an Orange County Top Doctor and Orange County Top Plastic Surgeon every year since 2014, was awarded "Best Facelift Doctor" in Los Angeles by CosmeticTown, and in 2018 was recognized as “Best Plastic Surgeon in the West” by Global Aesthetics.
