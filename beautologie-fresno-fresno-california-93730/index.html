He regularly travels internationally to India and countries in Africa and South America via his nonprofit organization, The Promise, to correct cleft lips and palates and treat burn injuries.

Dr. Shah lives in Malibu and enjoys traveling, exercising, and continuing his education in his free time, as well as spending time with his wife and two children.
