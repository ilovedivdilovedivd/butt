The main types of brazilbutlift

There are three ways to eliminate sagging of the skin and give your silhouette the desired contour:

  1. Surgical excision, which may be accompanied by liposuction in the presence of fat deposits or by installing gluteal implants in the case of lack of volume;

How is performed brazilbutlift?

Surgical lifting is performed under general anesthesia and takes about 3 hours. There are some basic techniques. For the patient the whole difference is in the arrangement of postoperative scars:

  1. horizontally at the top of the buttocks;
  2. diagonally across from the side to your groin;
  3. on the gluteal furrow.

The most popular is the last option. The inevitable scar will be safely covered by a natural fold. In other cases, it is much more difficult to hide. After applying the cuts excised the excess skin, and the remaining tissue tightens and straighten. At the final stage is applied standard cosmetic seam. The recovery period is quite long, the most uncomfortable part of it – the first 2-3 weeks after surgery when the swelling of tissues is preserved.

Then you can gradually return to usual daily activities, with the exception of sports, from which it will be necessary to abandon for 2-3 months, until the body is not fully restored.

Then it will be possible to assess and overall aesthetic result, which will remain for life – unless, of course, will not happen significant changes in body weight or a hormonal background.

For the Butt Thread Lift enough local anesthesia and it takes no more than 1 hour. Using a special needle according to a previously planned route, the thread entered under the skin, then “cling” to the soft tissues using special cones, hooks or notches.

Next the surgeon tightens the upper end of the thread, and with it all the sagging tissue, then fixes them in a new position. Recovery is very easy and takes no more than a few days, however, and the result is stored relatively short time – in 1-2 years it will be necessary to repeat the procedure.
