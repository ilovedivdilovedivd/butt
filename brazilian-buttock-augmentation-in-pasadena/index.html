How the operation is performed?

You need to pass medical tests to determine the health of the patient. It is, basically, a general analysis of urine and blood, an electrocardiogram, coagulogram, fluorogram and blood chemistry.

Bum implants are installed under the superficial fascia or under the gluteus maximus is most often; subcutaneously – never and very rarely-intramuscularly.

Gluteal implants have a different shapes:

Round implants provide a uniform increase in the buttocks, but their shape often looks unnaturally.

Anatomical implants allow to achieve absolutely natural look of gluteal region. Installation of implants requires a thorough pre-training, but the result is worth it.

Indications for buttock enlargement:

  1. congenital or acquired defects of the buttocks;
  2. the fat deposits in the buttocks;
  3. a sagging skin;
  4. small volume of buttocks and drooping of buttocks;
  5. atrophy of the buttock tissue.

Contraindications:

  1. obesity caused by disruption of the endocrine system;
  2. diseases of the cardiovascular system;
  3. bleeding disorder;
  4. liver and kidney diseases;
  5. diabetes;
  6. oncological diseases;
  7. acute viral disease;
  8. diseases of the internal organs in the acute stage;
  9. under 18 years old.

The recovery period

In total, the recovery period lasts about six months, after which the patient can live a full life without any restrictions. In the recovery period is necessary to strictly observe the recommendation of a doctor, in order achieve of good results.

The patient is in the hospital under the supervision of medical staff during the day after the operation, after which it discharged home.

The first 2 weeks will have to limit itself in many ways: you can not drink alcohol, smoke, play sports, go to the bath or sauna, do sudden movements and sit.

The first 2-3 months are required to wear a compression garment both day and night, in order avoid of complications. The physical activity and playing sports can be resumed after 3-6 months. Thus it is necessary to exclude a large load on the buttocks.
