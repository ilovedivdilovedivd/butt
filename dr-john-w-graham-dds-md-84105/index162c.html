Acceledent treatment. My orthodontist told me to use it twice a day once in the morning and onc
Acceledent more than once a day
Apr 22, 2016
While using Acceledent more than once a day is beneficial for reducing orthodontic discomfort, it will likely do nothing to further accelerate the rate of treatment. There are currently no studies evaluating this scenario in orthodontics, but we know from the orthopedic literature that maximum results come from 20 minutes a day.
Can maxilomandibular surgery work for sleep apnea?
Maxillomandibular Surgery for Obstructive Sleep Apnea (OSA)
Apr 22, 2016
In a word yes, maxillomandibular advancement is just about 100% effective for the permanent treatment of OSA. The diagnosis is critical however, and you should (if you haven’t already) have a sleep study to confirm that your sleep apnea is obstructive in nature and not central. Central sleep apnea isn’t treated by surgery.
