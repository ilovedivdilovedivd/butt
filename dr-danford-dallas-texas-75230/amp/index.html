What are some cystic acne treatments that don’t involve taking oral meds?
non-oral acne treatments
Dec 24, 2019
Intermittent cystic acne is likely hormonally driven and you would likely benefit from oral spironolactone. If you want to avoid oral medications then one option may be PDT therapy or Blue light therapy. This is typically used for actinic keratosis (pre-cancerous lesions) but is also used for acne. It is unlikely insurance will pay for this but may be a good option for you.
