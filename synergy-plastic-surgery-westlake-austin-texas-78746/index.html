Ankur Mehta, MD

Name: Ankur Mehta, MD
Statement: Dr. Ankur Mehta is a board-certified plastic surgeon specializing in enhancement of the face, breasts, and body. At his boutique practice in Austin, Texas, he frequently performs breast enhancement surgeries, tummy tucks, liposuction, and complete mommy makeovers.

At Synergy Plastic Surgery and the Synergy Medspa, Dr. Mehta and his colleagues also offer a full menu of nonsurgical treatments, including wrinkle relaxers Botox and Dysport; dermal fillers like Juvéderm and Restylane; CoolSculpting and CoolTone, for body contouring and muscle tone; and skin rejuvenation procedures like laser resurfacing and chemical peels.

Dr. Mehta is a detail-oriented plastic surgeon with an artistic eye and strong desire to help others. In fact, he was drawn to the field of aesthetics because he understands first-hand how appearance can play a role in one’s self-confidence. When Dr. Mehta was younger, he was often teased about his nose. He wasn’t aware that there was anything he could do about the size of his nose until his mother took him to see a plastic surgeon for a rhinoplasty.

This procedure was life-changing, not only for his confidence but because it drew him to a career as a plastic surgeon. Dr. Mehta says that nothing makes him happier than seeing his patients experience life-altering procedures that boost their confidence to off-the-charts levels.

Dr. Mehta is truly invested in the happiness and satisfaction of each patient. To achieve satisfying outcomes, he’s highly involved with his patients from beginning to end, including the consultation, pre-operative care and guidance, the operation itself, and all post-operative care. He even gives his patients his personal cell-phone number. This involved and hands-on approach has made Dr. Mehta a highly sought-after plastic surgeon in the greater Austin area.

Dr. Mehta obtained his undergraduate degree in psychology from the University of Texas at Austin. He then completed his medical degree at the University of Texas Medical Branch at Galveston, where he also completed his residency. He then completed a fellowship in burn reconstruction at the University of Texas Medical Branch at Galveston with Shriners Hospital for Children before going into practice.

Dr. Mehta is board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery. He has received membership into the American Society of Plastic Surgeons and the Alpha Omega Alpha Medical Honor Society.

Dr. Mehta has also received numerous awards since beginning his work as a plastic surgeon. He is listed in the RealSelf 100 and has earned five different Vitals awards, including Patient’s Choice Award, Compassionate Doctor Award, and On-Time Doctor Award.

With nearly a decade of experience in the field of plastic surgery and a deep passion for helping his clients, Dr. Mehta is eager to help you meet and surpass your aesthetic goals. Whether you’re seeking breast enhancement, a mommy makeover, a tummy tuck, a Brazilian butt lift, or another plastic surgery procedure, book a consultation with Dr. Mehta at Synergy Plastic Surgery to begin your journey.
