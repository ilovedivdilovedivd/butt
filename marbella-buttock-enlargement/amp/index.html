Procedure brazilian butt lift

At the time of surgery, the patient must be healthy (including it concerns an influenza, catarrhal diseases, etc.)

The patient should undergo a medical examination:

  1. general blood analysis,
  2. general urine analysis,
  3. blood type, Rh factor RW discs, HIV, hepatitis B and C,
  4. blood biochemistry (glucose, urea, ALT, AST, bilirubin)
  5. analysis of blood coagulability,
  6. electrocardiogram,
  7. chest X-ray.

10 days in advance before the surgery, it is advisable to refrain from smoking, taking drugs containing aspirin.

In the evening, before the surgery, you can take a bath, it is possible a light dinner.

Marbella butt augmentation surgery is performed under general anesthesia and lasts for 1.5-2 hours. The shape and size of the pre-selected implant depending on the shape. During the operation, the implant is usually placed under the gluteus maximus muscle through a small (about 5-6 cm) incision in the crease of the sacrum between the buttocks.

Furthermore, the pockets are formed under the muscle for the installation implants. Subsequently it is not visible cicatrixes. The fabrics over implants are mapped surgical sutures, the skin is sutured cosmetic suture.

One more a method is the installation of implants, under fat, usually in the upper part the buttocks.

Sutures are removed 12 days after surgery. In 2 weeks after surgery, you can begin to everyday lifestyle, with some restrictions. Physical activity is recommended after 6 weeks.
