Neck Lines In Young Adult

Is it possible to add length to the chin without adding projection?
Adding Length To Chin Without Projection
Jan 30, 2021
This is very common concern with an easy solution, as dermal filler will most likely be your best course of action. Dermal filler is carefully injected into the outter portions of the chin, as well as along the jawline to help widen out the chin, and fill in areas that are lacing volume. The beauty of it all is that it’s a non-permanent solution, and therefore something you can easily dissolve if you decide you miss your old chin.
