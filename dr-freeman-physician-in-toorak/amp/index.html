Different fillers
Apr 6, 2018
Thanks for your question.
It is okay to use different dermal fillers in your lips. Most only last about 6 months in the lips and even if there was product still there, it is okay to use a different one the next time. It is good for you too as you may develop a preference for the feel and look of a certain filler, although the final result of the filler will also be injector dependent…
