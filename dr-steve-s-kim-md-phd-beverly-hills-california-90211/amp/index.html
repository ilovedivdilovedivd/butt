Creases and Scar Line After Asian Bleph / Ptosis Repair
Swelling after asian blepharoplasty
Apr 27, 2011
Swelling from asian eyelid surgery (especially from the incisional type) may take as long as 6 months to fully recover.  A lot of the swelling will come down by 3 to 4 months.  But final results with all of the swelling coming down and the incision line fully softening up takes 6 months.  Recovery is much quicker for the non-incision technique.
