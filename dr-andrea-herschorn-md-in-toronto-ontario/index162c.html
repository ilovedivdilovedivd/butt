Does exercising regularly shorten duration of Botox?
Exercise and its effect on your Botox treatment
Nov 10, 2017
Regular moderate exercise does not seem to affect the length of time your Botox lasts. However, if you are someone who forces her facial movements while exercising, such as frowning while lifting weights, this may wear off your treatment effects a bit sooner. Since exercise is so good for you, I don’t advise that you stop your activity for the sake of a few more days of Botox effects! Thanks!
