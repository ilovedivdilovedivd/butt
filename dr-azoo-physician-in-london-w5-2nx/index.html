Is it possible to fix asymmetry without getting fuller lips?
Lip asymmetry
Oct 13, 2017
The underlying cause of the asymmetry can be investigated by seeing photos before and after the initial filler injection and comparing them to the current ones 2 months post procedure. It is early for the filler to start dissipating at 2 months, even the older Juvederm Ultra range should last at least 6 months. A small amount of carefully placed filler can even out the sides without creating too much increase in the overall fullness of the lips.
