Filament Lifting (butt thread lift)

Butt thread lift is a secret of Brazilian aesthetic surgeons. Now, with the help of special threads, by a special technology without incisions and scars, just half an hour drooping buttocks are tightened and become elastic, rounded, seductive right before your eyes.

In the future, if desired, the procedure can be repeated. According to the testimony, Butt thread lift is successfully combined with fat grafting (lipofilling).

Depending on the body, the presence of fat, is selected installation method: over the gluteus maximus; under the fascia of the gluteus maximus; it is also possible intermuscular or axillary placement.

Liposuction + fat grafting (liposculpture)

The combination of liposuction techniques and fat grafting (liposculpture) allows to increase buttocks in Australia with the help of the patient’s own fat cells.

As a result of this approach, you can achieve double aesthetic effect: the volume increases in the required place, and in places of the fence of adipose tissue – excess body fat on the sides or belly is reduced. The fat is taken from the zones where there are excess fat: the abdomen, waist, back, and other locations.

Modern technology called Micro Fat Grafting: fat cells are transplanted by very small portions, in the form of beads arranged in a row, in layers. So in this case it is not just about the correction of the buttocks, but also to establish a new body contouring. It thus created the famous “Brazilian buttocks”.

The professionalism of the surgeon in this situation takes on special significance. Only careful execution of a set of strict requirements for the procedure will preserve the viability of of adipose tissue, which in turn will provide its reliable survival.

Indications:

  • hereditary structure: flat, small buttocks, for which is necessary to give the volume;
  • sudden weight loss, which caused a decrease of fat and sagging skin;
  • age-related changes, which involve the buttocks ptosis, the elasticity of tissue deteriorates, there is sagging skin.

Contraindications:

  1. acute diseases of internal organs or exacerbation of chronic diseases;
  2. blood diseases;
  3. diabetes;
  4. disruption of the endocrine system, which has led to obesity;
  5. diseases of the cardiovascular system.
