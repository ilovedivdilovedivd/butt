Statement: Dr. Steven Goldman is a plastic surgeon and the founder of Beachwood Plastic Surgery and Medical Spa, with offices in Beachwood and Westlake, Ohio. Specializing in mommy makeover procedures, breast enhancements, and rhinoplasty, he is one of a handful of surgeons who is double board-certified in both Plastic Surgery and Otolaryngology - Head and Neck Surgery.

Dr. Goldman and his incredible staff are committed to excellence in every aspect of the patient experience. “It’s crucial that your surgeon has the scientific perspective to select safe, effective surgical techniques that meet your face and body goals—and the artistic aptitude to individualize the procedure for your face, body, and personality,” he explains.

Dr. Goldman earned his medical degree from the University of Pittsburgh School of Medicine, where he stayed on to complete his internship in Surgery, followed by a residency in Otolaryngology. From there, Dr. Goldman headed to the University Hospitals of Cleveland and Case Western Reserve School of Medicine in Ohio to complete a residency in Plastic Surgery. He served as the hospital’s interim Chief of Plastic Surgery until 2006, when he left to launch his own practice.

Board certified by the American Board of Otolaryngology - Head and Neck Surgery and the American Board of Plastic Surgery, Dr. Goldman is a member of the American Academy of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery, the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery, and the American Society of Plastic Surgeons. He is also a fellow of the American College of Surgeons.

Dr. Goldman is highly regarded in the plastic surgery community and has been the recipient of numerous awards, including the Top Doc award from Northern Ohio Live Magazine.

What is the cost of Cellfina?
Cellfina cost varies depending on how many areas are treated
Jun 30, 2020
Cellfina cost generally varies from about $3000 to $6000, depending on how many areas are treated. The procedure is done under local anesthesia. Patients generally go to work the next day and can workout in a day or two. It works better on dimples than streaks or linear cellulite, but can improve both. Find a board certified plastic surgeon who's office has good experience with this technology. Good luck. 
Can I still use MiraDry if I've had underarm laser hair removal?
Miradry eliminates underarm sweating in most patients
Jun 30, 2020
We've had very high satisfaction rates with Miradry in our patients. About 2/3 do two treatments. Patients with excessive sweating (hyperhidrosis) or normal but bothersome sweating can use Miradry. Laser hair removal does not interfere with Miradry (although Miradry can result in some hair reduction along with sweat reduction). Our nurses or estheticians can use a starch iodine test to identify sweat glands if needed. Miradry is perform under local anesthesia in the office. Good luck.

How Many Botox Units for 11's or Frown Lines?
20 units is the most common Botox dose to the 11's.
Mar 31, 2020
The most common doseage of Botox to the glabella (the 11's) is 20 units. Patients with stronger muscles, especially men may require more, but in fact many patients use less to keep this area synchronized with the forehead, crow's feet and other areas. Dysport dosing is different. The most common Dysport dose to the glabella is 50 units, which is equivalent to 20 units of Botox in this area for most patients.
