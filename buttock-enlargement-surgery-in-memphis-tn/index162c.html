You need to collect all the necessary documents before the gluteal lift. The patient must give the conclusion of the therapist that he is completely healthy, as well as bring are the forms with the blood tests, urinalysis, coagulation and cardiogram.

The patient is required to stop smoking 10 days before Brazilian butt lift in Memphis, consume alcohol and medications which structure includes aspirin.

Before the buttock enlargement is performed general anesthesia to avoid the of painful sensations. Anesthesia can introduce intravenously or epidurally. A qualified anesthetist selects the best mode of anesthesia.

In advance, the surgeon determines the most efficient method of introducing the implant.

The surgeon draws special pencil lines on the patient’s body. The surgeon makes an incision along the lines. Their length is from 4 to 5 cm. Implants are installed in a small incision. Then the incision was sutured.
