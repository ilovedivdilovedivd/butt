Plastic surgery buttocks in Canada

Brazilian butt lift in Canada is a surgery for changing the volume and shape of the buttocks, which is usually done by installing implants. The intervention can be carried out for women and men.

In most cases, its purpose is aesthetic correction of the buttocks, but there may be a medical condition – reconstruction at the congenital defects or trauma. When talking about surgery, it meant an increase in the buttocks, as their reduction can be performed using other methods (liposuction, facelift, etc.).

Buttock implants

Buttock implant surgery in Canada – installation of implants under the gluteus maximus or under the fascia (connective tissue sheath). Placement directly under the skin is considered to be a mistake, because it can lead to migration (displacement) of implants or their rupture.

The structure of the implant is largely similar to those for mammoplasty, but has some differences. Firstly, they are much stronger than the breast because in a sitting position will experience severe stress.

Greater strength is achieved by increasing the thickness of the silicone shell (about 1 cm) and filling by viscous gel-elastomer, which has a “shape memory” feature. The physical properties of implants allow to approximate them to the texture of natural human tissue.

Another feature of the implants for buttock augmentation – the shape and dimensions. They can be round (for a homogeneous and uniform increase in the buttocks), or anatomical, which have complex shapes and are used for the individual “fit” for the patient. Sizes range from 150 to 500 ml.

In any case, from the surgeon requires a large precision in the placement and orientation of the implant, since improper installation will not provide the required increase in volume or lead to distortion of the shape of the buttocks.

Indications for Canada buttock augmentation

Gluteal region – one of aesthetic centers of the human body. Taut and toned buttocks is a necessary component of a harmonious figure. When saggy or flat “the fifth point” – there are non-surgical ways to solve the problem – specially selected exercises that strengthen the muscles of the buttock, as well as push-up underwear, which visually increase in volume of buttocks and modeled buttocks.

However, not everyone has the time for systematic training, in addition, they can not help with severe asymmetry or defects.

Underwear push-up only creates a temporary illusion of prosperity. In such cases, brazilian buttock augmentation comes to the rescue.

Indications for the bbl surgery:

  1. The small volume of buttocks, especially when coupled with their omission.
  2. Asymmetry of the gluteal region (usually manifested in the uneven distribution of body fat).
  3. Atrophy (sharp decrease in weight) of muscle and fat tissue in the area (due to the nature or structure-borne diseases).
  4. Deformations (usually after some trauma or pathologies – e.g., following postinjection abscess).
  5. Excessive and stretched skin on the buttocks.

The operation can be done at almost any age.

Contraindications

Buttocks plastic surgery has the following contraindications:

  1. Severe diseases of internal organs (heart, liver, kidney, respiratory system).
  2. Decompensated form of diabetes and other endocrine problems.
  3. Oncological diseases.
  4. Bleeding disorders.
  5. Infectious diseases.
  6. Furunculosis any other pyoderma gluteal region.
  7. Serious allergic disease.
  8. Obesity endocrine nature.
  9. Pregnancy.
  10. Menstruation (a relative contraindication, usually the operation is transferred to a few days, because it increases bleeding of tissues during menstruation).

The preoperative preparation

On examination, of the patient, the doctor determines the amount of future interventions, produces the operating area and the layout, makes choice of the optimal shape and size of the implants. Anamnesis is done – information about past illnesses and injuries, the presence of hypersensitivity to the drug.

Conducted laboratory and instrumental examination:

  • Clinical and biochemical blood and urine tests.
  • Coagulation.
  • Study of serum to certain infections (RW, HIV, hepatitis B).
  • Fluorography.
  • ECG.
  • Inspection anesthesiologist.
  • According to indications – consulting physician, endocrinologist and other specialists.

In the two weeks prior to surgery should stop smoking because nicotine leads to a spasm of blood vessels and slow wound healing. The patient should renounce the use of alcohol and drugs that affect blood clotting (aspirin, anti-inflammatory, and hormonal agents, anticoagulants).

On the eve of the operation you should take a shower, is allowed a light dinner. The intervention itself is carried out on an empty stomach under general anesthesia.

Operation

Buttock augmentation starts with incision in the gluteal crease between the buttocks in the sacral area, the length of which is 5 – 7 cm.

Under the gluteus maximus muscle is formed cavity (pocket) in which the implants are placed. The doctor evaluates the symmetry of the buttocks and the correctness of their contours. Drains are installed, tissue are sutured in layers, is made the imposition of cosmetic seams on the skin, scars from which are practically invisible in the future.

Duration of surgery usually is about 2 hours. The patient remains in the hospital for a couple of days.

Recovery period

Immediately after the operation, a special compression stockings worn on the patient, which he or she will have to wear from 1 to 3 months. Its function – preventing the formation of hematoma, pain reduction and the formation of the right buttock contours.

Sutures are removed by 7 – 10 days, or they disappear on their own if using resorbable material. For a few days you need to stop taking the soul, about two weeks are not recommended to sleep on your back and sit. You are limited in: smoking and alcohol intake, thermal procedures (sauna) and physical activity for a period of 2 to 4 weeks.

After installing the implant, it will be impossible to perform an intramuscular injection to a patient in the gluteal region – now you have to do them in the area of the hip.

Complications

Brazilian butt lift in Canada can have the following complications:

  1. Hematoma or seroma (accumulation of blood and tissue fluid in the operating area). To avoid such troubles doctor should carefully monitor intraoperative hemostasis (stopping bleeding during surgery), to ensure adequate drainage (removal of excess liquid after suturing tissues) and compression after surgery.
  2. Suppuration of seams zone with subsequent wound dehiscence. Prevention – compliance with the rules of aseptic and antiseptic, compliance with contraindications (presence of infection, pustular skin diseases), sometimes with a preventive purpose, the patient receives a short course of antibiotics before and after surgery.
  3. Violation of sensitivity of the skin associated with damage of small nerves during surgery. The norm is restored within a few weeks.
  4. Offset implants. To prevent the problem, you must correctly select the shape, volume and placement of implants.
