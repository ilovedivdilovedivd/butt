Can i use makeup after i use adapalene gel?
Make-up fine while using adapalene
Apr 1, 2019
Hi,
Adapalene is in the class of topical retinoids (which retinol is also a member of), and yes it is fine to use make-up in the morning after appying adapalene at night. Use of topical retinoids can cause irritation (redness and peeling) as a side effect. If this occurs then use adapalene less often, for example every other night, and/or apply a light moisturizer prior to applying the adapalene.
