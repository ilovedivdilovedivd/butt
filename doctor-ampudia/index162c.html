Statement: Dr. Diego Ampudia, MD is a plastic surgeon and certified specialist in general surgery in Tijuana, Mexico. A highly-skilled plastic and reconstructive surgeon, Dr. Ampudia specializes in working with post-bariatric patients who require body contouring procedures after extreme weight loss.

To address the excess, sagging skin that often accompanies rapid weight loss, Dr. Ampudia offers a number of procedures. Together, an abdominoplasty (tummy tuck) and liposuction can help remove extra fat and skin on the abdominal region, while a neck lift removes visible signs of sagging around the jawline. A butt lift, one of his most popular procedures, offers the benefits of liposuction as well as butt augmentation through fat grafting. Dr. Ampudia also performs breast lifts, arm lifts, and thigh lifts.

Because everyone’s goals and needs are unique, Dr. Ampudia is dedicated to working hand-in-hand with each individual patient before and after surgery to ensure that they receive the best possible results.

Dr. Ampudia received his medical degree at the Universidad Autónoma de Guadalajara and later completed three additional years of training in reconstructive and aesthetic plastic surgery. He has been in practice for three years.

Today, Dr. Ampudia serves as Chief of Reconstructive Surgery at the Hospital General de Tijuana. He is certified by the Mexican Council of Reconstructive and Aesthetic Plastic Surgery.
