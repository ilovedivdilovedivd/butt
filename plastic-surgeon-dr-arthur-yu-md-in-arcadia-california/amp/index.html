Does liposuction cause fat in other areas later on?
Liposuction Causing Fat
Nov 30, 2017
Liposuction per se does not cause new fat deposit in the other areas in the future. However, if you gained weight, then there might be more fat deposited into the other areas, because the pure fat cell numbers in the liposuctioned areas are already reduced. Consequently, the cells in the treated areas will take up less fat!
