Michael M. Omidi, MD, FACS

Name: Michael M. Omidi, MD, FACS
Statement: Dr. Michael M. Omidi, MD, FACS is a double board-certified plastic surgeon practicing in Beverly Hills and the Greater Los Angeles area. He is considered one of 10 best rhinoplasty surgeons both by Forbes and Cosemtictown Journal. RateMDs ranked Dr. Omidi #1 of 250 plastic and cosmetic surgeons in Beverly Hills for 6 consecutive years.

Dr. Omidi has been featured in Forbes, E Entertainment’s hit show “Dr. 90210 and MTV. He is the inventor of the Mannequin Lift™️or Mannequin Breast Lift™️, a procedure used by many surgeons around the world. Dr. Omidi offers a range of surgical and non-surgical treatments including mommy makeover, skin tightening, abdominoplasty, rhinoplasty, and breast augmentation.

“We believe that every patient is beautiful. Our goal is to enhance that beauty.”

Dr. Omidi received his medical degree at Saint Louis University School of Medicine in Missouri. He finished his postgraduate training at Northwestern Memorial Hospital in Chicago, where he served as chief resident, and completed a craniofacial surgery fellowship at Miami Children’s Hospital in Miami, Florida.

He is a fellow of the American College of Surgeons and a member of the American Society of Maxillofacial Surgeons.
