Bra size after implants – will my bra size get bigger or smaller in the months after surgery?
Breast shape never finalizes
Jun 5, 2023
Generally breast shape never finalizes for a lady. After a couple of weeks may be one or two months after isolated breast augmentation breast will loose surgical oedema (swelling) and may get a bit smaller. But we do not expect it to get smaller after that. Still breast can go down to a lower position if you have large implants like 625 cc. This is normal but it is named “ptosis or sag” not “involution”.

Is it safe to donate eggs once you’ve had a breast augmentation?
I would suggest you to postpone breast augmentation after successful collection of quality eggs.
Jun 5, 2023
Harvesting eggs for donation is performed with laparoscopical procedure and needs general anesthesia or at least sedation. 4 weeks after breast augmentation seems a good choice. From another viewpoint, although it is a cosmetic procedure, subjected surgical stress may interfere with ovulation and ovulatory hormones. Eggs are limited in number for each woman so I would suggest you to postpone breast augmentation after successful collection of quality eggs.
