Statement: Dr. Navnit Haror uses the most advanced tools and techniques and provides an advanced, modified FUE procedure that produces outstanding results.

Dr. Navnit Haror is a Gold Medalist in dermatology and works along his team of sugeons on all hair transplant procedures at his clinic in National capital of India, New Delhi. Dr. Haror performs hair transplant surgery exclusively and operates on only one or two patients per day. Dr Haror and his team perform both FUE (follicular unit extraction) and FUT (follicular unit transplant).

Dr. Haror performs a modified version of FUE referred to as Ultra Receptive FUE Hair Transplantation or UR FUE. During this technique, follicular units are extracted using a micromotor and implanted simultaneously. He Invented this technique in 2014 in Singapore and since then he is delivering best possible results with natural hairline and high density.

Dr. Haror possesses over 12 years of experience performing hair transplants and has been performing all techniques of hair transplant surgeries since 2011. He specializes in FUE (UR FUE), BHT (body hair transplantation), eyebrow transplantation, eyelash restoration, beard transplantation, moustache transplant, scar repair and other scar repair procedures, etc. He also provides medical treatments for managing all stages of male and female pattern baldness.

Dr. Navnit Haror has the staff and experience to perform large, densely packed sessions of ultra refined follicular unit hair transplantation exceeding 4000 to 4500 grafts when appropriate for the patient. Typical session sizes range between 2500 and 4000 grafts. Giga sessions may exceed 5000 grafts for patients with an optimal donor area without damaging the donor area.

Dr. Navnit Haror use a micromotor called the MM Marathon with punch sizes typically ranging from .8 mm to 1 mm depending on the bald area and the size of the graft. This reduces trauma and resultant scarring to the skin while causing no damage to the surrounding hair follicles. Dense packing typically ranges from 42 to 56 grafts per square centimeter (FU/cm2) which Dr Haror feels provides an adequate density close to natural one while preserving complete blood circulation in the scalp.

Dr. Haror uses all the latest and advanced techniques including the trichophytic closure technique for FUT or strip procedures, microscopic dissection and minimally invasive tools to reduce trauma and maximize density.

During FUE and FUT procedures, Dr. Haror pre-makes all recipient slits or incisions and then grafts are simultaneously harvested and implanted into tiny recipient incisions ranging from 0.7 mm to 0.9 mm. By simultaneously implanting the grafts immediately after harvesting, grafts spend no time outside of the body, minimizing desiccation/dehydration to the grafts and providing perfect yield.

To create the most natural looking hairline and result, Dr. Navnit Haror picks only the best quality 1 haired follicular units for placement in the first two rows of the hairline.
