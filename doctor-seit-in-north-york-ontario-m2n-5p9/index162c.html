Alternatives to Botox for my forehead lines?
Forehead lines can also be improved with dermal fillers
Apr 6, 2021
Forehead lines can also be improved with dermal fillers to improve volume and contour of the forehead. Fillers can also be place directly into the skin and dermis to improve the skin strength and improve the lines. Other alternatives include PRP to improve the collagen loss in the skin. Laser and radio frequency micro needing can also improve the collagen and texture of the skin to help minimize the lines on the forehead.
What can I do about the uneven smile lines on my face?
Definitely can be improved
Apr 6, 2021
Your smile lines and asymmetry can definitely be improved with some facial dermal fillers in the smile line and possibly in your cheeks to lift your face. We can also use some other technologies like radio frequency and microneedling or high intensity ultrasound to melt some fat and tighten skin to help achieve your goals.
How to make my face more symmetrical?
Our faces are normally slightly asymmetrical.
Apr 6, 2021
Our faces are normally slightly asymmetrical. They are sisters and not twins. Definitely can help with dermal fillers placed strategically. This the simplest way non surgically to improve symmetry.
Am I a candidate for under eye fillers?
Definitely
Apr 6, 2021
Definitely possible to improve your under eye appearance with under eye fillers, you will probably need your cheeks to be also injected to get the best results. Surgical option is also a consideration as well.
Would non-surgical jawline contouring be beneficial?
Some advice, please read!
Apr 6, 2021
You have a touch of laxity and some hollows around the chin, jawline area. Proper placement of some dermal fillers would create a smooth tighter contour to your chin , jawline area.
Can I wear a chin band whilst having chin fillers?
Not necessary
Apr 6, 2021
The chin band is not necessary for use with chin fillers. May affect shape of the fillers if the fillers are newly injected and the filler injections may not last as long from the constant pressure and manipulation to place the chin band on the face.
Can you advise on under-eye hollows, fine lines and new creases?
PRP treatments
Apr 6, 2021
Your under eye hollows and creases can be treated best with a combination of properly placed dermal fillers specifically formulated for the under eye area in combination with PRP treatments. Alternative treatments can include laser and radio frequency micro needling treatments to improve the collagen and texture of the skin in the area.
