What is a “lip flip” filler injection?
Lip Flip
Sep 9, 2021
A “lip flip” is an injection along the top lip to weaken the muscle to create a more “curved” look. It is not done with filler, but instead a neuromodulator (Botox, Dysport, Xeomin).
It is different as a lip flip relaxes the muscle on the upper lip, where fillers actually fill the area and replace lost volume.
