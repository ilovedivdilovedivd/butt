Are Lumps Permanent After SculpSure?
“Fat lumps” post SculpSure
Jun 7, 2017
While rather uncommon, lumps can occur post Sculpsure. Fortunately these will resolve over time, but in our clinic, we have seen a few people with these lumps for up to 3-4 months post procedure. Be sure to continue massage to the areas for 5-10 minutes twice daily to help them go away quicker.
Can I take pain medicine with SculpSure?
Pain medicine for SculpSure procedure
Jun 7, 2017
I would agree that the arms can be a sensitive area to treat with Sculpsure. I absolutely agree that Tylenol is a great choice. However, I would not recommend any anti-inflammatory medication, like Ibuprofen, Naproxen, or Aspirin, as they may potentially lessen the inflammatory response from the treatment and make the treatment less effective.
Can fat cells regenerate?
Can fat cells come back after SculpSure?
Jun 7, 2017
No. Once the Sculpsure treatment is complete, the fat cells are permanently destroyed. The same thing happens with Coolsculpting.
Three moles and hyperpigmentation, are there other options besides Halo?
Skin lesions
Feb 5, 2017
Halo is an excellent laser treatment, best suited to help with pigmentation, sun damage, fine lines and wrinkles, pore size.
Because of the laser’s fractionated technology, it only treats a small portion of the skin. It will not remove moles or other skin lesions.
Your best bet would be to have the lesions excised by a plastic surgeon or a dermatologic surgeon. Best of luck to you.
