Buttock augmentation implants is carried out under general anesthesia. By increasing the buttocks with silicone implants, incision is made in the crease between the buttocks the size of 4 to 6 centimeters.

Surgical wound is sutured using intradermal cosmetic seam. Antiseptic applied to the incision area. After the surgery patient wear a compression garment which should be worn for 4-6 weeks after Brazilian butt lift .
