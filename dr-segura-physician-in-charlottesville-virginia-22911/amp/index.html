How to fix vertical line that appeared after Restylane Kysse lip filler?
Lip support
Jan 22, 2022
It looks like you are lacking support around the lips. When you fill your lips, the valley between your lips and cheeks gets larger and creates that crease. I would suggest cheek filler to help lift the cheek a bit and decrease the amount of nasolabial fold and tissue in that area. You can also fan filler above the lip to support that area and fill in the crease.
Can Restylane Kysse be used for under eyes?
Would not advise
Jan 22, 2022
All fillers have different properties, G prime, cohesivity, moldability, etc. The tear trough (under eye) also comes with it's own set of difficulties when using filler such as, tyndalling (blue haze under the eye from filler), lymphatic draining issues. A great filler for under the eyes is classic restylane. That is probably the most commonly used filler for that area.
Is it true that I should not bend down after Xeomin injections?
Yes and No
Jan 16, 2022
Refraining from bending down is only for the first 24 hours as your provider is wanting to keep the Xeomin where it was injected. After that, you are free to bend down and kiss your dogs!
Xeomin, Botox and Juvederm don't last on me. Why is that?
Fast metabolizers?
Jan 16, 2022
Longevity of toxins and fillers vary from person to person. You may simply be a fast metabolizer. Patients who work out or simply have a quick baseline metabolism tend to see this.
Is it common to not see any improvement three days after Xeomin?
Absolutely!!
Jan 16, 2022
Xeomin takes the longest to see full results out of all of the toxins. I recommend waiting the full 2 weeks to see how you will respond.
10 units of Xeomin for 11s 3 days ago, no change
Too little Xeomin
Jan 16, 2022
The minimum dose to the glabella region which causes the 11's should be 20 units. It's my opinion that your injector under dosed you. With that being said, Xeomin takes 2 weeks to take full efficacy. You should wait for 2 weeks and then be seen for a touch up and to reach the minimum 20 unit dose.
Is Xeomin worth a try for people who already gained antibodies to Botox?
Yes
Jan 16, 2022
Xeomin lacks the accessory protein that leads to antibody formation. It is worth a try to see if you respond to Xeomin.
