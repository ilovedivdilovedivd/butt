Should Invisalign trays fully cover whole tooth?
Invisalign teeth coverage
Feb 19, 2019
Hello,
The clear aligners should fit properly and feel like they are snug to the bottom of your teeth. Full tooth coverage on your front teeth may vary depending on your teeth size. You may be best served by asking your dental office for a review with the dentist on your fit. Invisalign is great at making adjustments but you need to see a doctor to determine best fit.
