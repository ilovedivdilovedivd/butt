Buttock Augmentation Cost

There are two different ways in which the buttocks can be augmented. The first is with fat transfers to the buttocks, augmenting the buttocks with your own fat.

Liposuction is performed and then the fat that is harvested from the liposuction is injected into the buttocks.

The other way to augment the buttocks is using buttock implants. Silicone implants specifically made for the buttocks are implanted to add volume and create a better shape. Both procedures cost about $6,500-$7,000 in our practice.

There are positives and negatives to both and some patients are better candidates for one over the other. The average cost of buttock augmentation will depend on the technique chosen, (fat grafting, implants, or both), your anatomy and goals, the length of the proposed surgery, the number of areas being suctioned (if the chosen technique involves fat grafting), and the reputation and expertise of the surgeon (like other professionals, plastic surgeons’ fees will reflect their reputation and expertise, as well as the geographic area in which they practice).

Because there are so many variables, it would be difficult to give you an accurate estimate of costs, but in our practice augmentation of the buttocks might cost anywhere from $5000 (for the simplest of augmentation procedures) to $14,000 (both fees include all anesthesia and facility fees).
