To tight belly is not so difficult – the complex is so simple that it can be performed anywhere. Even in the office during working hours. The main thing – to exercise regularly and conscientiously.

Crunches is a basic set of exercises for the abdominals of brazilian tummy tuck workout. Crunches are the basic set of exercises for the formation of the abdominal, mainly aimed at stimulation of the rectus abdominal area.

Crunches create a beautiful embossed abs. This is the most common abdominal exercises. Lie on the floor, legs lifts up on the bench, tilt your head forward and, reducing muscle abdominals, bend forward, trying to pull up the ribs as closely as possible to your pelvis.

During execution, the shoulders and upper back are leaving the floor and rise upwards. You do not need take your lower back off the floor. If the exercise seems too easy, take in hands barbell (on the chest or behind your head).

Reverse Crunches gives the same effect as the previous exercise. But the movement is the opposite – bring your knees up to your face. Many athletes truly believe that this exercise will help them to pump up the lower abdominal

Do you want a flat stomach? Try to do crunches. They are very similar on lifting of body, with the only difference that by doing crunches, you lift only the upper part of the back, and not the entire body. Thus isolated abdominal muscles and thigh muscles are not involved in the exercise.

Lateral crunches used for the formation of a narrow waist. The main load is carried by oblique abdominal muscles. The technique of lateral crunches: the exercise is performed on the floor or on a bench. When performing lateral crunches it may also be present assistant that will capture your feet.

You should lie down sideways on the bench. Part of the body should protrude over the edge so to be able to to bend the body both up and down. Bend the body at the waist, slowly lower yourself down, and then slowly return back. The same exercise is carried out and on the floor: lie on your side, rest against elbow on the floor, and begin to slowly raise and lower pelvis.

Towards the end of course Leandro shows such exercises like planks. Exercise involves several muscle groups. And changing the position of the arms and legs by experimenting with the number of points of support, you can pump up the abs, arms, back, buttocks, and even a large number of different muscle groups.

And the most pleasant thing that for a this exercise does not need special conditions. At home or in a hotel room or in the park – wherever there is a flat area for support, you can do the plank.
