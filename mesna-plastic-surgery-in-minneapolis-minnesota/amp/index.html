Gregory Mesna, MD, FACS

Name: Gregory Mesna, MD, FACS
Statement: Dr. Gregory T. Mesna is a board-certified plastic surgeon and the founder of Mesna Plastic Surgery in Minneapolis, Minnesota. The founder of Mesna Plastic Surgery, he has expertise in a broad range of surgical options, minimally invasive procedures, and nonsurgical treatments for cosmetic enhancement of the face, body, breasts and skin. He specializes in breast enhancement, abdominoplasty, blepharoplasty, and liposuction but is best known for performing mommy makeover plastic surgery in the Twin Cities.

Dr. Mesna and his team at Mesna Plastic Surgery are dedicated to helping their patients feel more beautiful, with natural-looking results that can range from subtle improvements to transformations. “We share an uncompromising commitment to our patients’ goals, the care that they receive, and the services that we offer,” Dr. Mesna says.

In addition to plastic surgery, his Twin Cities practice also offers dermal fillers, Botox, noninvasive body sculpting, laser skin resurfacing, and skin tightening procedures, among others. He also offers skin rejuvenation treatments like HydraFacial, as well as professional skin care.

A lifelong Minnesota resident, Dr. Mesna graduated with his bachelor’s degree in chemistry from St. Olaf College in Northfield, Minnesota. He then obtained two doctorate degrees from the University of Minnesotaone—one in dentistry, another in medicine. Dr. Mesna continued his medical studies with an internship and residency in general surgery at the University of Minnesota before deciding to specialize in plastic surgery.

Dr. Mesna completed his advanced training with a residency and fellowship in plastic surgery before opening his private practice in Minneapolis in 1996, where he has been helping patients for over 25 years.

Board-certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery, he is a fellow of the American College of Surgeons and an active member of the Minnesota Society of Plastic Surgeons and the American Society of Plastic Surgeons, which allows him to stay up to date on new innovations and techniques in the field of plastic surgery.

In his free time, Dr. Mesna enjoys coaching his children as they play a variety of sports.

Is it normal for your arms to still feel hard and bumpy after 3 weeks of armlift?
Results After Arm Lift
Mar 21, 2017
Swelling, tightness, lumps, bumps, numbness, etc. are common findings after arm lifts and other procedures, especially if you had liposuction performed at the same time. All of these findings can wax and wane during the recovery process but generally they tend to improve over time. Gently massaging these areas can facilitate the process but I would recommend checking with your surgeon to make sure that your incisions are healed well enough to begin the massaging.
