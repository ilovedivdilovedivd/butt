How long after getting a tummy tuck do the drainage come out?
Drainage post tummy tuck
Jul 28, 2023
This is a very common question during most tummy tuck consults. The answer would depend on if your surgeon used drains or not. Drains can help collect the fluid that naturally builds up after skin excision and liposuction. They would typically be removed after 1-2 weeks. I would suggest consulting with a board certified plastic surgeon to discuss your options. Most patients are able to return to light duty work after 2-3 weeks.
How will my new belly button look after tummy tuck if I am keloid prone?
Preventing keloids
Jul 28, 2023
Hi there and thank you for your question. Preventing keloids from forming can be very challenging. Specifically look for a board certified plastic surgeon and discuss with him/her steps that can be taken to prevent keloids or help the incisions heal well. Steroid injections may be helpful in some cases. It is also important to avoid UV exposure and to use silicone based scar cream during the postoperative peroid.
Best of luck to you!
