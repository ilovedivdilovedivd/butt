Brazilian lift cost

The average cost for a Brazilian Butt Lift (BBL) in the Omaha area is $6,500. Of course this varies on the individual and the amount of suction needed to collect the fat before the transfer.

This is an outpatient procedure performed under an anesthetic so there is no pain during the procedure. The recovery is about 5-7 days. (David Finkle, MD, Omaha Plastic Surgeon)

The thing about a BBL is that it is not one thing. It can be many different things based on your goals, your body, and your consultation with your surgeon. For some, it is about adding volume, for some it is about adding shape, for most, it is both.

The key in your situation is to find someone that you like and trust in an office environment that makes you feel comfortable.

This is a great operation in the right situation and in the right hands. (Jeremy Pyle, MD, Raleigh-Durham Plastic Surgeon)
