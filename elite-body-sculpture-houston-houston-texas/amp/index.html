Gregory Pisarski, MD, FACS

Name: Gregory Pisarski, MD, FACS
Statement: Dr. Gregory P. Pisarski is a plastic surgeon with over 22 years of experience. The founder of Gulf Coast Plastic Surgery in Lake Jackson, Texas, Dr. Pisarski specializes in a range of plastic surgery and skin rejuvenation procedures, including breast augmentation, tummy tucks, mommy makeovers, liposuction, dermal fillers, and laser hair removal. Triple-board-certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery, American Board of Facial Plastic & Reconstructive Surgery, and the American Board of Surgery, he aims to provide his patients from across the Houston area with exceptional medical care and aesthetic results.

“My philosophy is to provide my patients with the highest level of medical care available in an ethical, confidential manner,” says Dr. Pisarki. “I strive to stay current with the most advanced technology in cosmetic enhancement procedures. We’ll help educate you about your options, so you can feel confident about your decision.”

Dr. Pisarski earned his medical degree with honors from the University of Texas Health Science Center in Houston before completing residencies in both General Surgery and Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery at the University of Cincinnati Medical Center in Ohio. He went on to complete several prestigious fellowships, including one in Pediatric Burn/Critical Care at the Shriners Burn Institute in Cincinnati, Ohio and another in Cosmetic Surgery with Drs. Baker, Gordon, and Stuzin in Miami, Florida.

A member of Rotary International and Knights of Columbus, Dr. Pisarski believes strongly in the importance of giving back. Over the years, he has traveled to the Philippines, Mexico, and Nicaragua to volunteer as a reconstructive surgeon, treating patients with cleft lip, cleft palate, and other facial deformities.

Dr. Pisarski remains deeply involved in a number of professional organizations in an ongoing commitment to broadening his knowledge of plastic surgery’s newest techniques. He is a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons, the American Society of Aesthetic Plastic Surgery, the American Academy of Facial Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery, the Texas Society of Plastic Surgeons, and the Brazoria County Medical Society.
