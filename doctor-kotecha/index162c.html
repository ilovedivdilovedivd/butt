Is It Ok to Rub Eyes after One Month of Lasik?
Yes it is ok to gently rub eyes one month after lasik
Sep 8, 2013
It is ok to gently rub the eyes one month post lasik. By this time the flap should be healed down. You should be able to tell if your flap is dislodged or wrinkled by blurry vision. If unsure, please make a follow up appointment with your ophthalmologist.
Lazy Eye and LASIK?
Amblyopia and Lasik
Aug 27, 2013
You may be a good candidate for lasik surgery, but understand that the goal of lasik surgery is to get rid of glasses and contact lenses. It will not fix your lazy eye. In other words it will not make your lazy eye see 20/20 like your other eye.

The lasik surgery that is right for you would require a lasik consultation including knowing your prescription and corneal thickness.

Hope this helps.
