The buttock augmentation and gluteal lift are performed either under general anesthesia or under local (epidural, spinal) or combined anesthesia (local + general). The buttock fat grafting is usually performed under local anesthesia.

The buttock fat grafting is usually performed under local anesthesia. The duration of these operations is as follows: 40 min – buttock augmentation, 1.5 hours – gluteal lift and 30-40 minutes – buttock fat grafting together with liposuction of donor area.

Contraindications to the buttocks enhancement are:
