The youngest age to have a hair transplant surgery
Sep 21, 2018
We generally prefer not to perform a hair transplant surgery on patients less than 20 years old. Patients between 20 to 25 years old should be informed of the expected results before the procedure. The best candidates for a hair transplant surgery range between 25 years to 70 years.

The cost of hair transplant varies from doctor to doctor. Some doctors charge per graft while others do not. In Turkey, the cost generally ranges from 1€ to 2€ per graft. How Long Does Hair Transplant Last?
Is Hair Transplant Surgery a Permanent Solution?
Aug 17, 2018
Hair transplant surgery is a permanent solution only if the transplanted hair is taken from a safe donor area. After the procedure, you may take a combination of minoxidil, propecia and use a laser cap to delay or prevent the patient’s own or existing hair from further hair loss, especially in younger patients. You may not need an extra session if you protect your existing hair from further hair loss.
I cant seem to grow facial hair on my cheeks, what can I do to rectify this?
Beard Hair Transplant
Jul 31, 2018
Minoxidil may be a temporary solution. However, you should be aware that minoxidil helps with growth of vellus hair which may not be compatible with the beard hair. Therefore, for a more permanent solution, you may have a beard hair transplant surgery done. You should be checked for any hormonal or genetic diseases before the procedure.
Hair Growth After Hair Transplant
What to Expect After Hair Transplant Surgery
Jun 22, 2018
Generally, for the first 2 months, there is no growth in the transplanted area. Between the 2nd and the 4th month, you may start to witness slow growth. Between the 4th and the 8th month, hair starts growing faster. Then between the 8th and 12th month, 20% or less of the remaining transplanted hair will grow. Just be a little bit more patient, you will start to witness results in a few more months.
