Can filler be injected around the helix of the ear?
Possible but be careful of your expectations.
Mar 18, 2019
Hi There,
Filler can be injected into the area but usually only small amount each session and very carefully. There is an issue with the blood supply that needs to be considered and also the amount of support the cartilage provides to hold the filler in place. You may need multiple sessions over many month to get it right. The last thing you want is lots of filler put in there that weighs down the ears and causes a worsening charge to another part of the ear.

Hope this helps.

Dr Mitch
