Is swelling and pain normal 36 hours after Botox?
Botox usually results in minimal swelling. You may have a small bruise or developing infection.
Feb 13, 2019
Hello VAMom,
Usually, treatment with Botox does not result in significant swelling or pain. If it is localized to one specific site and not all of them, I would look out for either a small bruise (as there are numerous tiny vessels in the area) or redness of the skin, indicating a developing infection. Bruising will self-resolve over the course of the next couple weeks, but an infection might require oral antibiotics. If it continues to get worse, I encourage you to get the area reevaluated….
