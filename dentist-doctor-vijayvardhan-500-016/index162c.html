The type of crown that is fixed there is a PFM crown. Usually, PFM crowns look a bit unnatural as metals ( alloy used under the white ceramic) are opaque while natural teeth are translucent( allows some amount of light to pass).
To get excellent results ask your Dentist for metal free crowns that have translucency property similar to teeth. However, such crowns are very expensive in the US. You can discuss with your Dentist.
