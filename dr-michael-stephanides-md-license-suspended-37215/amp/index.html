I am having lipo done on my abdomen and inner thighs. How will I be looking and feeling 3 weeks
Recovery following liposuction
Dec 17, 2014
Full recovery following body and thigh liposuction usually takes at least 18 weeks. Initially there is significant swelling that slowly goes down over a period of 9 weeks. At the addressed areas are still firm. An additional 9 weeks will be required for the areas addressed to become soft. At 3 weeks you will be comfortable as far as pain/discomfort is concerned but you will not be at your final size.I hope this helps.Michael Stephanides, MD
