How long should I wait to get a breast reduction after breast feeding?
Breast reduction after breast feeding
Oct 17, 2014
I generally recommend waiting about 4 months after you have stopped lactating for a consultation. This allows your breasts to return to their final shape and size since there are many changes that occur during pregnancy and breast feeding. This will also allow your Board Certified Plastic Surgeon to evaluate you and determine what surgical procedure will give you optimal results.
