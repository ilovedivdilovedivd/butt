What’s the best treatment for nasolabial folds?
Deep nasolabial folds
Sep 3, 2018
Good skin care is always a great place to start. There are many components to the ageing face. Loss of volume in fat pads, ligament laxity and bone resorption all start in our early 30s. Injectable filler will help restore some of these issues. From your photos you appear to have lost some mid face volume in your cheeks which is contributing to your nasolabial folds. Replacing the volume in your cheeks is a nice place to start.

Can I do microneedling on top of my cheek fillers?
Microneedling after fillers
Aug 27, 2018
Microneedling is safe after treatment with fillers. It is safest to wait at least 2 weeks after your filler treatment before having your procedure. It won’t breakdown your filler as it does not penetrate to the same treatment area. Infection is a small risk due to protocol.
