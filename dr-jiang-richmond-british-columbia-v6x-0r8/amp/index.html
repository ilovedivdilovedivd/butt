Ulthera – Are Welts a Normal Side Effect?
Welts/Hives after Ultherapy
Sep 25, 2021
Hives and Welts can be a normal side effect due to the swelling after the treatment and should go away within a few days to weeks.
You should contact your provider or clinic if it still exists around 1 month.
Can Ultherapy Be Used Directly Under the Eye? Are There Alternatives?
Ultherapy under the eyes
Sep 25, 2021
Ultherapy technically can not be performed directly under the eyes, because the handpiece is not perfectly designed for eye area skin.
I recommend Thermage for tightening and lifting the eyelid, the depth and size of the eye tip is perfectly designed for the eye.
What is the Difference Between Ultherapy and Thermage?
Thermage or Ultherapy?
Sep 25, 2021
Thermage and Ultherapy both use energy to restore collagen production in the dermal layer of the skin. But the type of energy each treatment uses is different. Ultherapy uses ultrasound energy while Thermage uses radiofrequency energy.Ultrasound energy usually reach deeper into the dermal layer, even down to the fat and SMAS layer, to provide more dramatic and noticeable skin tightening and lifting.
Long Term Effects of Ultrasound Tightening/Ulthera on the Skin?
Only Long Term Benefits
Sep 25, 2021
Our clinic has been practised with ulthera technique over decades and has not noticed any negative long term side effects from the therapies yet.
In fact, many patients reported they get long term benefits through the treatment. Ulthera can help with reproducing collagen and increasing skin elasticity over years.
Should Ulthera Be Painful?
ways to manage Ultherapy pain
Sep 25, 2021
Most of our patients find the pain is tolerable. There are also many ways to manage the painfulness. The most common two methods our clinic offer are oral medication and pronox gas. Those methods are able to lower the pain comfortably through the treatment.
What Risks Are Involved with Ultherapy?
Essentially ultherapy is a total non-invasive treatment
Sep 25, 2021
The treated skin areas can sometimes become red and swollen in the hours or immediate after the procedure, and short-term sensations including tingling, swelling, and tenderness may occur. On rare occasions, there may be some bruising or numbness, but these typically go away within a few days to weeks of the procedure.
How Long Does It Take to See Ulthera Results?
Ultherapy result shows in 3-6 months
Sep 25, 2021
Don’t worry. Individuals will produce collagen at slightly different rates. This means that, although most patients’ results become evident between 3 and 6 months, you may see results earlier or later than this. In fact, you get the immediate 20% of the results on the same day and rest 80% results starting as early as 2 weeks to 3 months post treatment.
