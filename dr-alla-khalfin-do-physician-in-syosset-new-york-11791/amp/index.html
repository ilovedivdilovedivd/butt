How can I get faster results with truSculpt 3D and is there swelling for days after the procedure?
Swelling after Trusculpt
Sep 15, 2019
Hi, there is usually no swelling after Trusculpt treatment. The area gets hot and red right after procedure, but usually redness is gone after few hours. If you get swelling, usually goes away after 24 hours. However, you do not see results right away. It does take 4-12 weeks to see results. Drinking water helps, but does not mean that you will see results sooner.

Morpheus8 for darker skin tone
