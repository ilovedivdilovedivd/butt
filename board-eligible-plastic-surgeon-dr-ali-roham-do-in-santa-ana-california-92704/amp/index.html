How soon can I return to my facial laser hair removal sessions after rhinoplasty?
Laser Hair after Rhinoplasty
Jul 4, 2019
Hi There,
There should be no reason why you cannot get laser hair removal after rhinoplasty. You should definitely have them avoid the mid-face region (area around the nose) especially if your surgeon performed an osteotomy (broke your nasal bones.) Again I would confirm with your surgeon but as long as the laser doesn’t involve your nose, you should be in the clear. Hope this helps.
