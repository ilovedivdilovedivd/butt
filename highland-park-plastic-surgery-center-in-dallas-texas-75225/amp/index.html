Doctors

George A. Toledo, MD

Name: George A. Toledo, MD
Statement: Double board-certified plastic surgeon Dr. George A. Toledo is the founder of Highland Park Plastic Surgery Center in Dallas, Texas. Highly skilled in all areas of aesthetic plastic surgery, he is especially well-known for his expertise in rhinoplasty, having performed over 3,000 rhinoplasties over more than 36 years in practice.

Dr. Toledo has earned a reputation for both his technical skill and dynamic patient care. “Simply stated, I treat my patients the way I would expect to be treated: with respect, candor, professionalism, and compassion,” he says. “I would expect my surgeon to have the highest credentials and level of training possible and to be thoroughly experienced in performing my surgical procedure.”

After receiving his medical degree at the University of Texas Southwestern Medical School in Dallas, Dr. Toledo completed both an internship and residency in General Surgery at Baylor University Medical Center. He followed with three more residencies, including Otolaryngology – Head and Neck Surgery at Duke University Medical Center; Otolaryngology – Facial Plastic Surgery at Louisiana State University Medical Center; and Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery at the University of Texas Health Science Center, and the M.D. Anderson Cancer Institute in Houston, Texas.

Dr. Toledo is certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery and the American Board of Otolaryngology – Head and Neck Surgery. He’s a member of the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery and the American Society of Plastic Surgeons. He is also the recipient of numerous awards, including a Texas Monthly Magazine SuperDoc, Vitals Patients’ Choice Award, and Top Ten Dallas.
