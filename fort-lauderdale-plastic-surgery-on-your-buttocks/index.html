Essence of the method and indications for surgery

Brazilian butt lift Fort Lauderdale – method of surgical bottom lift using silicone implants.

By using this operation you can increase the volume of the gluteal region, to adjust her shape and to give the buttocks a more distinct contours.

The operation is carried out at the ineffectiveness of attempts to “to grow” the gluteal muscles through physical exercise and in order to eliminate age-related changes of the skin in the area of the buttocks (loss of elasticity, ptosis of the soft tissue).

Indications for bum plastic surgery

1. insufficient volume of the gluteal muscles and sagging buttocks ;
2. the desire to change the shape of the buttocks;
3. the displacement of the gluteal fold;
4. sudden weight loss, which reduces the amount of of adipose tissue and ptosis of the soft tissue in the gluteal region;
5. the presence of depressions on the lateral surface of an upper part of the thigh;
6. atrophy of the soft tissue of the gluteal region or the deformation which arises as a result of injury or any infectious diseases.

Types of gluteal implants

Gluteal implants are composed of a dense, thick of the elastomeric shell which is filled with cohesive a silicone gel.

Silicone is has “memory effect”is well simulates the natural shape of of muscles and does not spread if damaged. Produces two types of implants in volume 140-180 ml:

  1. round;
  2. anatomical (teardrop-shaped).

The surface of the implant is smooth and a textured. Size, amount and type of of the filler, doctor chooses based on common proportions of the body and the anatomiсal shape of the buttocks. The modern plastic surgeons are using a new generation of implants that are manufactured by the company Eurosilicone (France) or Silimed(Germany). They are completely safe for the human body and do not require later replacement.

Preparation and the method carrying out of plastic surgery buttocks lift

Preparation for the buttock enlargement includes an: consultation with a plastic surgeon, an anesthetist, a therapist and a medical examination in order to identify contraindications to the procedure.

Before you change the shape of the buttocks, the patient must pass the following analyzes:

  1. general and complete blood count;
  2. fluorography;
  3. electrocardiogram;
  4. coagulation (blood clotting test).

10 days prior to bum augmentation should stop taking of analgesics, and non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs, stop smoking and drinking alcohol (alcohol and nicotine thin the blood and cause the appearance of postoperative hematomas). Eating and drinking in the day of the gluteal augmentation are prohibited.

Buttocks enhancement is performed under general anesthesia or a spinal anesthesia, duration – 1,5-2 hours. A doctor with a scalpel makes a small (up to 5 cm) incision (in the area of natural skin folds), forms the pocket and installs implant. There are three methods for installing an implant:

  1. under the gluteus maximus – fillers the round form are placed volume up to 200 ml;
  2. under the lean muscle the fascia – subfascial implant placement, at atrophy of the gluteus maximus;
  3. intramuscularly (between the musculus gluteus maximus and the musculus gluteus medius) – complex, but the most reliable method of implant installation.

The doctor, after installation and fixation of the implant, the skin sutured a cosmetic suture, installs a drainage and imposes a pressure bandage on the wound.

How is the recovery period?

The recovery period lasts 4-6 weeks (depending on the characteristics of the organism). In a hospital a patient spends 2-3 days. Sitting and lie on your back for a week after the butt augmentation is prohibited (it can cause rupture of the implant), doctors recommend to rest lying on his stomach or side.

The patient, a while will to feel discomfort during walking (pain, burning), but the symptoms are disappearing after healing of surgical wounds. Sutures are removed in 10-12 days, and the drainage removed in 2-3 weeks (when the edema of come down). The final result after the butt augmentation will be noticeable after 2-3 months.

Postoperative recommendations:

  1. within a month after the butt augmentation you must wear a compression garment (pants, shorts);
  2. 5 weeks you can not play sports, access to the sauna or swimming pool;
  3. order to reduce recovery period is necessary to give up smoking;
  4. makes injections in the area of the buttocks is prohibited because it can cause rupture of the implant (if necessary make injection in the thigh area).

Possible complications and contraindications.

Contraindications for the procedure:

  1. heart disease;
  2. renal and hepatic failure;
  3. coagulation disorders;
  4. endocrine obesity;
  5. diabetes;
  6. pregnancy;
  7. oncology;
  8. blood diseases.

Possible complications:

  1. hematoma or seroma (is most often caused when you install of textured implant);
  2. allergic reaction to silicone (individual intolerance of the material);
  3. bleeding (a result of damage of the blood vessel);
  4. the development of infection;
  5. the formation of the expressed of keloid the scar.
