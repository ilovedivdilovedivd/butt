Best cartilage option for revision rhinoplasty?
Best Cartilage Option for Revision Rhinoplasty
May 14, 2018
There are three sources of cartilage. Septal that is Flat, Ear that has curved shape and rib.

For most reconstructions if the septum was not operated before, I would use as first option septal Cartilage, the second option is ear cartilage that can be modified to a different shape with small incisions. And rib cartilage is only used for big defects.
