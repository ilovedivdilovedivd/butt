The recovery period after butt augmentation

The patient is in a hospital within 1-2 days after the gluteal augmentation. During this period are possible morbid sensations in the operation zone and a slight increase in temperature.

You can not sit on the buttocks 1 week after surgery, in the next 2 months you need to wear a compression underwear. It is forbidden to make injections in the gluteal region. Injections are allowed to do only in the thigh area.

Possible complications after butt augmentation

Complications after surgery in Brooklyn are rare and most often belong to the general surgery (bleeding, pyesis wounds, the formation of rough scars).

Contraindications for butt augmentation

These include blood clotting disorders, cardiovascular disease, diabetes, chronic diseases in the acute stage.

In addition, the operation is contraindicated in obesity caused by metabolic disorders.
