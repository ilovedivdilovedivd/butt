Is hair transplant the last answer for female pattern hair loss?
FUE hair transplant is a permanent solution
Aug 21, 2015
Hair transplantation is nowadays a solution also for female hair loss as there are many cases of women requiring density or lowering the hairline. The FUE technique is the only suggested restoration technique due to its minimally invasive character and for the natural results achieved without scarring or pain (effects of FUT). Apart from the transplantation extra care should be taken for the preservation of the existing hair through the regular use of minoxidil and PRP (monthly).
How many hair grafts do I need?
Hairline design
Aug 17, 2015
The hairline area is really important to look dense and natural. In order to achieve these two objectives the hairline design must be implemented really carefully and according to specific measurements. Density must also be achieved through maximum extraction of grafts that would surpass 2000. More thorough examination is required, though.
1 month post hair transplant, safe enough to go for PRP treatment?
PRP does help
Aug 17, 2015
The PRP is considered to be one of the most important conservative treatments for the preservation of the existing hair. It is actually recommended the day of the session and afterwords under monthly protocol for the following three months. It is repeated after six months. Studies support it.

Any suggestions for sudden hair loss at 20 yrs old?
Check many factors before you seek any treatment
Jul 31, 2015
Sudden hair loss happens non only in older men, it may also concern younger ones. There are many explanations for this problem. Hereditary hair loss seems to be more prevalent than ever before. So I would advise you to check your nutritional and lifestyle habits before you ask for treatment.
