Polythurane breast implants?
Polyurethane Implants
Dec 3, 2013
Polyurethane implants have not been available in the US for nearly 20 years. No one ever proved conclusively what happened to the polyurethane but my personal experience was that the polyurethane remained in the breast pocket in a soft capsule when the silicone implant and shell was removed. I always left the soft capsule intact and placed a new silicone-shelled implant in the pocket and never had a problem.
