Brazilian butt lift in Florida

Brazilian butt lift in Florida – is a correction of the buttocks shape using silicone implants. Indications for this surgery may be the asymmetry of the buttocks, distortion of the shape after sudden weight loss or childbirth, atrophy of the muscular layer, buttocks deformation after the injury.

At the disposal of plastic surgeons are round and oval (anatomical) implants. Implants are made using the latest technology of high-strength material, which prevents damage to the implant during use. The casing of the gluteal implants textured, thus reducing the risk of fibrosis (hardening of the buttocks). A special gel is used as a filler, simulating the natural softness and elasticity. In addition, the gel structure is such that even damage to the shell of the implant does not lead to leakage of the filler.

High aesthetics of surgery is achieved through the use of modern technologies. Buttock enhancement is done through a small, between four and eight centimeters, the incision, that located in the crease between the buttocks. This allows you to hide the incision almost completely.

Brazilian butt lift in FL is a full surgical procedure, so you necessarily need a medical examination, aimed at identifying possible contraindications. Contraindications include: diseases of the internal organs in the stage of decompensation, blood disease, reduced blood clotting, diabetes, endocrine system diseases, heart disease, malignant neoplasms.

Buttock implants are installed under spinal anesthesia or, if indicated, under general anesthesia. Brazilian buttlift surgery is made by forming a “pocket” under the gluteus maximus muscle, where the implant is embedded.

This technique allows you to get the best aesthetic result. Surgery ends by stratified suturing of tissue by using a resorbable suture. The wound is closed with a sterile bandage.

There may be pain in the early postoperative period, which is usually stopped by administration of analgesics. The first day after surgery the patient is recommended only lie on his stomach. The drains are removed on the second day, and the patients are issued to outpatient treatment.
