The director and founder of Providence Plastic Surgery & Skin Center in Charlotte, North Carolina, he provides patients with a range of procedures including breast augmentation, breast lift, tummy tuck (abdominoplasty), liposuction, mommy makeover, and Brazilian butt lift.
