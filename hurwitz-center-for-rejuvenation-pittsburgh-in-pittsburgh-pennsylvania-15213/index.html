Doctors

Dennis Hurwitz, MD, FACS

Name: Dennis Hurwitz, MD, FACS
Statement: Dr. Dennis Hurwitz is a board-certified plastic surgeon and founder of the Hurwitz Center for Plastic Surgery in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. With over four decades of experience, Dr. Hurwitz has expertise in a wide range of surgical and nonsurgical cosmetic procedures for the face, breasts, and body. He has positioned himself in the fields of plastic surgery and obesity surgery as one of the most respected practitioners or established techniques and an innovator of safe, effective advancements.

He is particularly well-known for his breast augmentation, facial rejuvenation, and body contouring procedures, including his signature Vertical Facelift and Total Body Lift. Over the course of his career, Dr. Hurwitz has helped thousands of patients redefine their image by lifting a great weight off their shoulders, literally and figuratively.

Dr. Hurwitz received his medical degree from the University of Maryland Medical School and completed his residencies and training at prestigious universities and hospitals. “When I found out about the artistry and high skills necessary for plastic surgery, and the type of patients that come for help, I knew it was for me,” he says. “It’s an incredible opportunity to take healthy people and bring them to a level of aesthetics and self-esteem not previously possible.”

Dr. Hurwitz is board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery. He is a member of both the American Society for Aesthetic Plastic Surgery and the American Society of Plastic Surgeons, as well as a fellow of the American College of Surgeons. For the past 20 years, Dr. Hurwitz has been recognized by Castle Connolly as one of America’s top surgeons.

He is currently a clinical professor of plastic surgery at the University of Pittsburgh Medical Center, where he trains senior residents of plastic surgery. “I like my new patients to understand that I’m coming from a private practice with a high academic interest,” says Dr. Hurwitz. “They are coming to a plastic surgeon who is not only innovative but also a great teacher. I also innovate new techniques and technologies, so my patients will have the latest techniques that are tried and proven through my operative experience, and I report this to plastic surgeons around the world.”
