Statement: Dr. Levant Acar is a medical aesthetics doctor practicing in Istanbul, Turkey. Dr. Acar specializes in hair transplants—including scalp, beard, mustache, and eyebrow procedures—and uses various methodologies, including FUT (Follicular Unit Transplantation or the trip method), FUE (Follicular Unit Extraction), DHI (Direct Hair Implantation), and DHI Sapphire Hair Transplant.

To stay abreast of the latest innovations and developments in the hair transplantation realm, he participates in top-level educational seminars across the world, as a student and lecturer.

This breadth of knowledge and expertise in hair transplantation makes Dr. Acar a sought-after expert in his field by patients and peers. Additionally, he has been a recipient of the Patient Service Awards every year since 2014.

After graduating from Istanbul University Capa Medical Faculty, he participated in Dr. Horst-Schmidt-Kliniken (HSK) Medical Education Programs, to further hone his knowledge of FUE hair transplant, laser therapy, and medical aesthetics. Separate from HSK, he completed advanced training programs in mesotherapy injections, dermal filler, and cosmetic gynecology.

Prior to opening Cosmedica, his hair transplant and plastic surgery center, in Istanbul in 2011, Dr. Acar served as the medical director of the Medical Aesthetic Unit at a private international hospital. He is fluent in English, German, and Turkish, which allows him to engage and work with a diverse spectrum of patients.
