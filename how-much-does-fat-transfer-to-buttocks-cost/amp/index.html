How Much Does A Brazilian Butt Lift Cost?

In Hawaii, a Brazilian Butt Lift costs between $6,000 – $12,000. The buttocks is a very interesting area of the body. In most areas, if you suck out fat, the skin shrinks up and the area becomes thinner. In the buttocks, if you suck fat out, the butt falls down. Fat grafting to the buttocks is much safer, simpler, and with fewer complications than the placement of silicone implants in the buttocks area. Fat grafting to the buttocks can increase the lift of the butt and its posterior projection, but to get the beautiful S-shaped curve requires liposuction of the waist and muffin tops, then liposuction and re-grafting of the lateral thighs.
