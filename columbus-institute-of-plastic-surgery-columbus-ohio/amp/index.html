Tip Rhinoplasty: What Is It?
Tip Rhinoplasty
May 3, 2016
Tip rhinoplasty is when you only work on the lower part of the nose (the part that is wider then the middle of your nose).  Sometimes, that is the only part that people are unhappy with.  They may want a better shaped tip narrower tip, cute little narrow tip, etcTo do that, it usually means making a tiny incision and improving the shape of your nose.Good luck!!
How much sooner will a wound heal with a wound vac instead of the wet to dry?
Wound Healing with a Wound VAC
May 3, 2016
In general, a wound of the size you are showing will take about 6 – 8 weeks to heal with a Wound VAC.  It would take about 10 – 12 weeks with W – Dry dressing. Also, increasing protein intake, a balanced and high caloric intake,and no infection will speed things up considerably.
