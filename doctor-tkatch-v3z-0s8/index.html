Preparing your skin for C02 laser resurfacing

Is it safe to wax after IPL sessions?
Safe to wax after IPL?
Nov 26, 2021
Yes, you are safe to wax until you feel safe enough to access your aesthetic provider for IPL or laser hair removal sessions. You will need to stop waxing however, before you resume your IPL treatments as they target the hair follicle and waxing will effect your results. Best, Dr. Tkatch

Sex after 71!
Nov 26, 2021
So glad that you are interested in enjoying intimacy in your 70's, that's awesome! If you previously were able to have intimacy before menopause without discomfort then MonaLisa Touch can be very helpful with addressing menopausal changes in vaginal tissue health. Best, Dr. Tkatch
Does weight gain effect facial fillers?
Weight gain after Fillers
Nov 26, 2021
Fillers placed in April 2019 will generally have dissolved within 12-18 months, so long gone. If you are experiencing water retention (puffiness), speak to your family doctor to ensure your thyroid is functioning well and you have no metabolic issues. Your family doctor can also help refer you to a nutritionist and/or discuss prescription medications to help you lose weight. Best, Dr. Tkatch
What is the effectiveness of vaginal tightening?
Mona Lisa C02 Vaginal Laser
Nov 26, 2021
Mona Lisa C02 vaginal laser is excellent for treating genito-urinary syndrome of menopause and lichen sclerosus, If you are interested in vaginal tightening seek out an experienced gyneocologist who can offer multiple vaginal technologies including radio frequency, which is excellent at achieving vaginal tightening. Fotana is an erbium laser treatment that may give some vaginal tightening effect along with treating genito-urinary syndrome.
Is it safe to do microneedling with PRP in between eMatrix treatments?
Safe to do PRP treatments with Ematrix
Nov 26, 2021
Ematrix is a radiofrequency skin resurfacing treatment that will help with your acne scars, as will the microneedling and PRP treatments you are doing. No problem to alternate these two treatments and likely to result in great results. You may want to consider having the treatments a little further apart though as skin takes time to remodel after either of these treatments. 4 weeks apart would be fine. Best, Dr. Tkatch

Tear trough filler treatment
