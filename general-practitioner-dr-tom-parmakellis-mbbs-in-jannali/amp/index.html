Can I get nasolabial folds filler?
Hyaluronic Acid Filler
Aug 9, 2020
Nasolabial folds can be filled with hyaluronic acid filler. The filler is best placed DEEP into the fold to reduce the depression in this space giving you a more youthful appearance. The procedure is not painful and using local anesthetic cream or nerve blocks can make it even more comfortable. Product can be inserted with either a needle or cannula. I prefer cannula injections as I believe they are safer and less likely to cause bruising.
