How to increase the buttocks by an operation?

The operation are performed under general anesthesia or spinal anesthesia approximately 1.5-3 hours.

Buttlift surgery is performed in the several stages:

In the crease is made a small incision between the buttocks. The butt implant is installed through the incision made under the gluteus maximus muscle or under the fascia of the muscle. The implant is not placed under the skin because it is impractical and inefficient.

After the installation of the implant, tissues are mapped and closed with stitches. The incision is sutured a special bio – resorbable thread, which dissolve after a week. If you are using non-absorbable sutures, the doctor removes their in about 2 weeks. An important rule of buttock enlargement: implants in the buttocks should not go beyond the boundaries of the cavity, in which are placed, so the cavity must always be smaller than the area of the gluteal muscles.

Implants for the buttocks are very similar to implants for breast augmentation. They consist of a silicone shell and the a gel of the filler (excipient can be saline, or silicone gel).

Brazilian butt lift in Dubai is carried out to achieve a more aesthetic appearance of the buttocks.

Indications for surgery:

  1. human desire to change shape and increase the size of the buttocks;
  2. the presence of atrophy gluteal tissue (eg, as a result of diseases, injuries);
  3. ptosis of the buttocks;
  4. the asymmetry of the muscles;
  5. insufficiently developed buttocks muscles.

Contraindications

The butt augmentation is not performed if the patient has the following diseases:

  1. diabetes;
  2. coagulation disorders;
  3. obesity;
  4. acute infectious diseases;
  5. disruption of the endocrine system;
  6. diseases of the cardiovascular system.

The recovery period

After buttocks enhancement, the patient must be in a hospital in 1-2 days. The first few days after gluteal augmentation, are observed painful sensations in the operated area, increase in body temperature and a decrease in sensitivity. When severe pain is recommended to take painkillers. The discomfort and unpleasant sensations go through 2-4 weeks.

In the first two or three days you can not take a shower. Two or three weeks, you need to wear a compression garment. After the bum lift is forbidden to sit on the buttocks approximately one week.

By the usual way of life, you can return two weeks later, with the exception of physical activity.
Sport and physical activity, visit bath, solarium are banned for six weeks. The final result can be estimated four month later.

After the buttocks enhancement, it is recommended to lubricate scars the with a special ointment or cream for healing scars.

There is also a permanent restriction after the brazilian butt lift – are prohibited injections in the gluteal area.

Complications after bottom lift

Complications are infrequent. Possible emergence:

  1. general surgical complications: scarring, bleeding, abscess;
  2. the asymmetry of the buttocks;
  3. displacement of the implants;
  4. accumulation of serous fluid (seroma);
  5. in the case the displacement of the implant, pain and pressure on sciatic nerve.
