Why is the recovery time for facial fat transfer so much more than HA fillers?
Recovery from facial fat grafting versus fillers
Nov 28, 2018
Fat grafting to the face involves significantly larger volumes (20-60cc) when compared to injection of HA fillers (1-4cc). It also involves spreading out the fat into a larger area and into layers.  As a result, there is more swelling and bruising with fat grafting which can last 2-3 weeks.

Can my vagina be tight again? It's just gaping and loose now.
Vaginal Tightening
Jun 10, 2018
No matter what the cause of repeated vaginal stretching (child birth or sexual acts), eventually there can be either a disruption of the musculature of the vaginal opening or musculature that is stretched beyond its functional capacity.  These muscles can be repaired through vaginoplasty and reconstruction of the muscles at the vaginal opening.
