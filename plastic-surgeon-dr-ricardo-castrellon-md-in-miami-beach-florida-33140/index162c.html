Can Old Scars Be Improved?
Old scars can be improved by excision and delicate closure or by resurfacing
Jun 11, 2013
The process of scar maturation takes approximately a year after the injury. During that time silicone sheets , steroid injections and some creams might help improve scar appearance. However in old scars this are not an option. Scar revisions or resurfacing with lasers or dermoabrasion remain as therapeutic options especially in larger scars.
