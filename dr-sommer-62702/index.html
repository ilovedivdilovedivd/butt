When are permanent stitches after Benelli lift usually removed?
benelli lift permanent stitches
Apr 20, 2019
This is a good issue to point out when it comes to Benelli lifts. The lift that is created with this stitch relies on the permanent stitch so it is never taken out. It is possible that it may come out on its own or your surgeon removes it due to exposure or infection. If this occurs, you will likely lose the effect of the stitch and the areola will spread to a larger size.

The swelling of the lower eyelid is due to the botox weakening the lower orbicularis oculi muscle. This most commonly occurs when the lower eyelids are injected along with the crows feet. gynecomastia incisions
Apr 20, 2019
Scarring can be quite minimal with this level of gynecomastia. Often the glandular tissue can be removed through an incision around the bottom of the areola or a straight line through the areola and around the bottom edge of the nipple. I prefer the incision through the areola as it disappears in the natural irregularity of the areola. Your surgeon will be able to discuss the options with you and show you diagrams of the incision options.
