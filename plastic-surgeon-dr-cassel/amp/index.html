I take a blood thinner. Can I get BBL or tummy tuck?
Blood thinners and cosmetic surgery
Oct 12, 2014
There are many reasons to be on blood thinners. With the agreement of your medical doctor, I would have you discontinue the blood thinners for at least 7 days and not resume until 7 days after surgery. I do not use Lovenox with cosmetic patients, but I do ambulate the patients early and vigorously, especially when the surgery is in the area of the pelvis. I do not operate around the pelvis on patients with history of thrombophlebitis or pulmonary embolus.

Sep 28, 2014
You do have tubular breasts. The bases are quite constricted and there is a wide flat space between the breasts.
