Doctors

Nicholas Adams, MD

Name: Nicholas Adams, MD
Statement:
Dr. Nicholas Adams, MD is a Board-Certified Plastic Surgeon who is also specialized in reconstructive and hand surgery with the Center for Plastic Surgery, PLLC. His primary focus is Plastic Surgery of the face, body, and breast.

Dr. Adams grew up on a farm in Grand Forks, ND, where he also received his B.S. and M.D. from the University of North Dakota. After graduating with Alpha Omega Alpha (AOA) honors, Dr. Adams completed a 6-year, integrated, plastic surgery residency at Michigan State University where he trained with world-renowned faculty specializing in aesthetic and reconstructive surgery. Following plastic surgery residency, Dr. Adams completed the Hand, Upper Extremity, and Microsurgery Fellowship at the University of Arizona in Phoenix.

Dr. Adams is committed to restoring and enhancing form, function, and beauty for each guest through a personalized and curated approach. He provides the highest quality of care and results through cutting-edge, contemporary techniques as well as a warm and welcoming environment. In addition, Dr. Adams believes in a 360-degree approach to guest wellbeing, satisfaction, and safety, and takes pride in developing and maintaining life-long relationships with his guests. Dr. Adams’ approach to medicine is building trust and educating his guests about the options available to address their specific goals and needs. This team approach allows for joint decision-making to get each guest to their desired goal through the smallest amount of surgery or least invasive techniques necessary.
