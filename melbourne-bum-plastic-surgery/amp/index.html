Anatomy structure gluteal muscles

The gluteus maximus is the largest surface of the three gluteal muscles. It constitutes a large part of the shape and appearance (buttocks).

It is the only one of the gluteal muscles, which is not attached to the protuberance of femur and is attached to the body of the femur. They are are massive, have surprisingly rough structure, and are arranged in parallel with each other on the rear surface of the pelvis.

Under the large muscle is located gluteus medius muscle, further gluteus minimus muscle.

Gluteus medius muscle – it is thick muscles of triangular shape, disposed on the side surfaces of the pelvis and also partially hidden under a large muscle. Well developed, they form a beautiful line of the hips.

Small gluteal muscles – flat muscle located under the gluteus medius muscle. They are arranged similarly to gluteus medius muscles. Are attached to the pelvis and the front side surface of the femur.

Preparation for the operation and progress of the operation

The main task of any plastic surgeon is preserve the health of the patient. After passing the required tests happens consultation with the surgeon or anesthetist, which with his verdict on the state of your health and allows surgery. When all is well, the doctor assigns the date of lifting buttocks. And the preparatory process begins.

Before the operation, you are warned that you need and how should you prepare for the surgery:

  • The day before the surgery, it is desirable to use a lighter products, exclude heavy and fatty foods. In the morning, if the operation is scheduled for a later time, you can eat a yogurt, and drink sweet tea. Nothing more.
  • Do not take into account that it is allowed for 6 hours before the procedure of anesthesia and surgery. This is done in order to avoid unwanted complications during anesthesia.
  • For smokers, it is desirable to exclude smoking for 2 weeks before surgery. Smoking is not only adversely affects the general state of health, but also at the healing of tissue, the appearance of necrosis.
  • Limit alcohol for a week prior to surgery, it is recommended for the same reason as smoking.
  • A few days or weeks before operation do not take analgesic, antipyretic drugs, like aspirin, it may increase the risk of bleeding. If you are taking any medications, tell your surgeon or anesthesiologist, which consults you before the surgery.

Installation of buttock implants performed surgically, and, therefore, under general anesthesia. Sometimes surgeons do under local anesthesia, but it is an exceptional case.

General anesthesia is performed as follows:

  1. intravenous anesthesia;
  2. endotracheal anesthesia;

Placement of the implant is:

  1. under the gluteus maximus muscles,
  2. or sub-fascial, above the muscles.

The surgeon makes an incision about 2-3 cm long at the crease of the buttocks. The scar will be invisible and placed in the crease between the buttocks.

If your case of implant placement is under the muscle, the incision will be made in the crease of the buttocks so that the scar will not visible. Then, the pocket is formed under or over the top of the gluteus maximus, in which the implant will be placed. After the installation of the implant the surgical sutures are applied.

Within about 2 hours, surgery is completed, although the duration may be more, depending on the complexity.
