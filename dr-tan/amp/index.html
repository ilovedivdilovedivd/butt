How Long Does Botox Last?
Botox longevity
Mar 24, 2020
Botox lasts on average 3-4 months, although there is large variation from 2-6 months, depending on your metabolism and number of units injected. Results and longevity is usually dose dependent, ie. a higher dose will likely produce better results and lasts longer. In some patients, repeated treatments causes muscle atrophy, and also trains the treated muscles to be less active, meaning a longer duration of results, or a lower dose can be injected.
