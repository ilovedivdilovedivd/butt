The average cost for a Brazilian butt lift, depending on the region of the United States, performed by a board-certified plastic surgeon in an accredited facility is approximately $6,000 to $12,000.

This costs includes surgeon fees (for liposuction, fat processing, fat transfer, and body scultping), surgical facility fees, general anesthesia fees, and postoperative care.

It is possible, as for any cosmetic surgery procedure, to find someone willing to do it for less, however, these providers are often not board-certified plastic surgeons, do not have the proper training, and may not perform surgery in an accredited surgical facility.

It is also possible to travel abroad for plastic surgery procedures at a reduced price, but again, these procedures are often performed in facilities which are not held to the same regulatory standards as those in the United States. (Jaime Perez, MD, Tampa Plastic Surgeon)

Price Should Not Be the Deciding Factor

It is important not to sacrifice your safety for price, because it can cost you dearly later – either as embarrassment from poor results, the cost of revision surgery and in some cases even your life. Brazilian Butt Lifts are comprised of two components: liposuction and fat injections.

In order to have smooth, natural results you will want to find a doctor who has extensive training in plastic surgery and particularly this procedure. When considering the price of the procedure, patients need to remember that the doctor needs to pay for an anesthesiologist, nurse, medical staff, supplies, etc.

So if the price of the procedure is “too good to be true”, it probably is. Although safely performed everyday, it is important to remember that this is a surgical procedure and should be taken seriously. At our practice we only work with board certified anesthesiologists, registered nurses and trained medical staff to ensure safety at all times. During your consultation, be sure to ask your surgeon about his surgical staff and specialty training. (Tarick K. Smaili, MD, Beverly Hills Plastic Surgeon)
