Can vascular occlusion happen with Ellanse and other permanent fillers?
Vascular Occlusion
Apr 2, 2020
Hello, vascular occlusion is a risk with any filler agent used. This includes Hyaluronic acids, Ellanse, and Radiesse. These are not common complications. Ellanse and Radiesse cannot be reversed like Hyaluronic acid fillers Be sure your visit an experienced practitioner always!
