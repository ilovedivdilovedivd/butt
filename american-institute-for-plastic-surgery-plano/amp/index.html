William Alan Dulin, MD

Name: William Alan Dulin, MD
Statement: Dr. William Alan Dulin is a board-certified plastic surgeon practicing at the American Institute for Plastic Surgery in Plano, Texas. He has extensive experience performing complex surgical procedures, including rhinoplasty, breast augmentation, body contouring, and reconstructive surgery.

He provides the highest quality of care for his patients at every step of their journey, from consultation through their procedure and recovery. “My goal is to treat patients to make them as happy as possible,” says Dr. Dulin. “I want them to have the best outcomes we can to help improve their self-esteem as well as their outward appearance.”

Dr. Dulin’s commitment to his patients makes him one of Plano area’s well-respected surgeons. He takes the time to listen deeply to each patient and develop a comprehensive treatment plan, with honesty and transparency being paramount. “What sets me apart from other physicians is that I try to be as honest as possible,” he says. “I try to tell them honestly what can be done, whether it’s what they want to hear or not.”

After graduating from Henderson State University in Arkadelphia, Arkansas, Dr. Dulin received his medical degree from the University of Arkansas, where he garnered academic accolades. Following medical school, he completed a residency in general surgery at the University of Arkansas before relocating to Temple, Texas for a plastic surgery residency at the Scott and White Memorial Hospital.

Dr. Dulin is board certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery and a member of the American Society of Plastic Surgeons, the American Medical Association, the Texas Medical Association, and the Texas Society of Plastic Surgery. He has hospital privileges at the Medical Center of Plano, Presbyterian Hospital of Plano, Baylor Medical Center of Frisco, among others in north Dallas.

With over a decade of experience, Dr. Dulin prides himself on his ability to make patients look and feel their absolute best, and on providing exceptional care both before and after their procedures. “We try to make the consultation as comfortable as possible to put you at ease,” he says. “We have a discussion about the procedure and your expectations and go through the process of learning what we offer and what is possible.” His support of patients extends well past recovery, as he checks in periodically to ensure they’re satisfied with their results.
