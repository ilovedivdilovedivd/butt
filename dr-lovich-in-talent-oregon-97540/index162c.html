Ice after breast augmentation
Jan 29, 2016
I saw a patient 6 years ago who had breast augmentation by another surgeon, went home took a Percocet and put an ice bag on each breast. Surgery and pain medication can disrupt the protective sensation that normally occurs to protect our skin from thermal injury. I never recommend the use of ice at the site of a surgical procedure.
