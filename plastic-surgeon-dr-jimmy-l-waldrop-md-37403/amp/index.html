Which breast implants are best to achieve that high volume breast implant look?
Best Implants for Desired Outcome

To achieve the look you desire, the answer for the best implant option would be an overfilled round saline implant. This will offer the most rounded artificial look. Breast augmentation. Will losing weight make them smaller?
Lose Weight First
Mar 1, 2017
Depending upon how much weight you lose, your breasts may get smaller or sag. For this reason it is best to lose the weight before having the procedure to ensure consistent and lasting results.
How Can I Get Rid of Loose Sagging Skin After Pregnancy?
Loose, Sagging Skin After Pregnancy
Nov 21, 2016
Loose, sagging skin is a very common complaint following pregnancy. The two most common areas of complaint are the abdomen and the breasts. The best option to address the abdomen is in abdominoplasty. This approach allows the best result because it involves treatment of the excess skin, fat and muscle tightening. To address the breasts, one may require a mastopexy (breast lift) to tighten the lax skin. Often, an implant is also necessary to restore volume and size.
Liposuction vs Laserlipo? Which one is better?
Laserlipo?
Mar 19, 2014
That is a good question. Both options are good for anyone who is a liposuction candidate. The difference is that laser lipo uses a small laser attached to the lipo cannula in order to help melt fat and may also result in more skin tightening than classic lipo alone.
