Is it possible for a 280lbs female to get Lipo instead of a tummy tuck?
Lipo instead of tummy tuck in a 280lbs patient… NOT to DO
Feb 17, 2016
Liposuction can be performed on a patient that is 280lbs.  However, understanding what the patient’s expectations are is critical.  If the patient is trying to improve their ability to buy clothing,  or the patient has a particular area that they wish to address then it is not unreasonable to proceed with liposuction. Performing a tummy tuck on a 280lbs has significant risks associated with it and most Board certified plastic surgeons would probably discourage it.
