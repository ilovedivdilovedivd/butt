Gluteal augmentation in Minnesota

Brazilian butt lift in MN is a plastic surgery to correct the size and shape of the buttocks.

Plastic surgery on butt augmentation allows to acquire the desired shape and size of the buttocks by setting the silicone implants or by method of lipofilling.

As a result, you get raised and rounded contours, in accordance with the individual characteristics and the type of body structure.

Surgeons use all current methods of increasing the buttocks, including minimally invasive, via which the butt augmentation is performed with minimal intervention in the body.

This allows you to reduce the recovery period and the period of hospital stay, reduce the number of incisions and the risk of postoperative complications.

As a result, you get perfect shape and size of the buttocks.

Indications for Minnesota butt lift surgery:

– Ptosis of buttocks;
– Atrophy or deformation of soft tissues;
– Low volume of the gluteal area;
– Defects resulting from trauma.

Contraindications to the buttocks augmentation operation:

– diabetes;
– cardiovascular diseases;
– bleeding disorder;
– the presence of visceral disease in the acute stage.

The duration of the buttock enhancement in MN

The operation takes two to four hours. Minnesota brazilian bum lift is performed under general anesthesia, although in exceptional cases, may be carried out under epidural anesthesia.

The surgeon performs incision between the buttocks and if necessary, on the hip, to remove excess fat and create space for the implant.

Patients should not have to worry about the scars, they will not be seen after 2-3 months after surgery.

The most common location for implants, is formed under the gluteus maximus muscle. The wound is sutured in layers. Superimposed several layers of bioresorbable sutures.

Methods conducting the operation and the period of recovery

Butt augmentation is used when the patient wants add a little volume or adjust the shape of of the buttocks. During this brazillian bum lift, is introduced into the problem area, patient’s own fat, which is not rejected and hardly decreases in volume. Thanks to located in the adipose tissue, mesenchymal stem cells, the skin of the buttocks is rejuvenated and acquires a more youthful and smart appearance. This method is minimally invasive and helps to reduce the rehabilitation period.

If we are talking about a cardinal change of buttocks their shape or volume, for these cases, use silicone implants. The surgeon selects the shape and size, taking into account the individual characteristics of soft tissue structures and the patient’s wishes. The incision is performed in the natural crease between the buttocks, so that the incision turns completely invisible. Implants are placed in a special pocket, or under the fascia of the gluteus maximus.

Thereafter, the drainage is introduced, the incision was sutured, and the apply a bandage. For best results, often, plastic surgery on buttocks, is combined with the pullup the hips, or liposuction.

After operation

After surgery, in all patients will be swelling, bruising, numbness. Slight pain is inevitable after buttock enlargement. Swelling and bruising after 10-14 days pass. To minimize the probability of complications, patients will wear a compression garment.In the postoperative period, banned heavy exercise for 6 weeks after surgery. Full recovery occurs within 2-3 months.

In each patient, the rehabilitation period, proceeds differently depending on the characteristics of the organism. Within a month, you need to wear a compression garment and advised to limit physical activity. After 6-12 months, the final result is achieved.
