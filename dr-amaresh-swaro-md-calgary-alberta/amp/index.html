What Are the Post Treatment Instructions After Botox?
Post-Botox treatment instructions
Jul 13, 2020
I advice my patients to avoid strenuous activity and avoid applying makeup for 6 hours. I explain that strenuous physical activity or massage to face can cause displacement of the injected Botox leading to unwanted results and applying makeup can have risks of infection.

Can You Exercise After Botox or Will It Decrease the Results?
Exercise after Botox
Jul 13, 2020
I advice patients not to do strenuous activity but light exercise is safe. I also ask them not to wear makeup on the same day. I have found that many carry on with their usual day to day activity without any problems.
How Long Does Botox Last?
How long does Botox last?
Jul 13, 2020
It depends on how long has the reconstituted Botox been sitting for and individual person’s metabolism. Reconstituted Botox should be used with 8-12 hours of opening the bottle. Botox usually lasts anywhere between 4 to 6 months but I have seen if one continues to use Botox on a regular basis then muscle tone/strength reduces which can lead to less frequent injections.
