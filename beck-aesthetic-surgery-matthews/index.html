Joel B. Beck, MD, FACS

Name: Joel B. Beck, MD, FACS
Statement: Dr. Joel Beck, MD, FACS is a highly experienced, board-certified plastic surgeon based in Charlotte, North Carolina. With 18 years of expertise spanning both the Bay Area of Northern California and Charlotte, NC, he is renowned for his commitment to medical excellence and patient-centered care.

Dr. Beck's extensive training includes burn surgery research, a craniofacial surgery fellowship, and advanced plastic surgery and reconstructive training. This comprehensive background enables him to bring valuable insights into anatomy, spatial relationships, and healing to the realm of aesthetic procedures.

His specialties encompass facelifts, liposuction with fat transfer to the buttocks, rhinoplasty, breast augmentation, hair restoration, and transgender procedures. Dr. Beck's "No Rush" policy reflects his dedication to patient education and comfort, allowing individuals to make well-informed decisions at their own pace. He provides personalized care in a relaxed, private setting.

After earning his Bachelor of Science from California Polytechnic State University in 1992, Dr. Beck completed a surgical residency at the University of California Davis in 1999. His commitment to education continued with a Fellowship in Burn Surgery Research at UC Davis in 2000 and a Craniofacial-Plastic Surgery Fellowship, followed by a Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery Residency at the University of Mississippi in 2003. He is board-certified by the American Board of Plastic Surgery and maintains membership in prestigious plastic surgery associations, including the American Society of Plastic Surgeons and the American College of Surgeons. Dr. Beck is also a prolific author, with numerous articles published in esteemed journals such as the "Annals of Plastic Surgery" and the "Journal of Craniofacial Surgery."
