Postoperative period and recovery

Early postoperative period after brazilian buttock lift surgery accompanied by temporary painful sensations usually passing over several days. To speed up the recovery period and reduce the risk of complications in the postoperative period, you must comply with all recommendations of your doctor.

For everyday lifestyle with some restrictions, you can begin 10-14 days after surgery. Limit physical activity is recommended for 4 weeks. For sports and active physical exercise is recommended to start no earlier than 3 months after the operation.

To prevent displacement of implants within 3 months you can not sit on the implants (sit only on the ischial tuberosity). We do not recommend long-term (more than 20-30 minutes) sit for 3 weeks. The final exercise is recommended after 6-8 weeks.
