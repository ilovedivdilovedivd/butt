Priority kind of anesthesia for brazilian butts is the general endotracheal anesthesia. If the endoprosthesis replacement is shown, then the operation is performed under general anesthesia. When choosing other methods, surgeons can use spinal or local anesthesia in conjunction with sedatives.

Getting surgery: incision

Brazilian butt lift – a unique surgery that allows to install the implants through a small incision. The incision is made in the hollow between the buttocks in the upper right corner, through which the implants are installed. This incision allows you to get rid of the visible scars. After healing is complete, the incision will be completely invisible. Sometimes the incision may be made in the pre gluteal crease.

Installation of implants

Implants are installed through the cut made. In modern plastic surgery implants under the skin are not installed. They are arranged in several ways:

  1. inside muscles
  2. under the gluteal muscle,
  3. under the gluteal fascia.

These methods of installation have the following advantages:

  • muscle fixes the implant and prevent it from moving on the buttocks;
  • the most natural look and tactile feel;
  • an implant under the muscle reliably protected from infection, including postoperative.

Cosmetic bioresorbable sutures (threads) are imposed after buttock augmentation with implants. Sometimes, instead of threads used staples to close the wound. If necessary, you can install drains to prevent accumulation of fluid beneath the fabric.
