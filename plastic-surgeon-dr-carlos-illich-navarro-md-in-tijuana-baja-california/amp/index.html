Surgery with hyperthyroidism – Does this make me a bad candidate for a mommy makeover?
Hyperthiroidism and cosmetic surgery
Dec 13, 2022
As long as you hyperthiroidism is well controlled with the help of your endocrinologist, there’s no contraindication to perform cosmetic surgery. Special lab tests are mandatory to make sure thyroid hormones are stable, Having uncontrolled hyperthiroidism and undergoing surgery is what really places a patient at risk of serious complications.
Make sure you always go by the hand of your Medical Team (Plastic Surgeon, anesthesiologist and personal endocrinologist).

Having a mommy makeover – when should I have stopped the Mili birth control?
Stopping birth control before elective surgery
Sep 7, 2022
It is advisable to stop oral contraceptives 4-6 weeks before elective surgery to reduce the risk of a thromboembolic event (blood clots), however there are other postoperative measures to also help prevent it and the fact that your a healthy young woman, also diminishes the risk. Just make sure to tell your medical team (surgeon and anesthesiologist specially) about this detail, so they are aware and decide the proper measures to ensure your safety based on their medical criteria.
