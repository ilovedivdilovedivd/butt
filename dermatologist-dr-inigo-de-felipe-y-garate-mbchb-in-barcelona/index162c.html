Skin care products for both vulgaris acne and rosacea?
Cleansers for acne and rosacea: the difference
Jul 25, 2019
Dear Patient,
Both acne and Rosacea arise from too much oils produced in the sebaceous gland but while in the first case the surface of the skin is strong and thick, in Rosacea it is fragile and sensitive. Cleansers with powerful emulgents can irritate the skin of a patient with Rosacea. There is an easy trick to know whether a cleanser has a lot of detergent: shake it and see what happens: if it contains a lot of foam, it won’t be suitable for sensitive skin or Rosacea.
Best regards
