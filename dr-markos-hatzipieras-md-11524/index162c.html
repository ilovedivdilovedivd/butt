What is the best solution to get rid of a small dent 1.5 years after a breast lift with implants
Scar distancing
Sep 3, 2020
The reason might be scar hypotrophy (fat separation or hypotrophy under the skin).This may be treated with fat transfer (if the overlain skin has not thicken also). A scar revision may also treat the problem, but it may be necessary to be done on both sides in order not to disrupt symmetry.Fillers are not indicated as the may make the area look “blue” or lighter.

If you want to keep your volume intact and maybe also enhance your upper pole fullness at the same time, you might go for a lift and a very small implant.
