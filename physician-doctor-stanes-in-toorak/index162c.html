Does Hyaluronidase also dissolve some of your facial fat along with the filler?
Hyaluronidase does not dissolve your natural fat.
Sep 18, 2020
Hi faith2711,
Hyaluronidase is an enzyme that breaks down hyaluronic acid. It should have no effect on your natural fat tissue. It can break down your natural hyaluronic acid, however this is usually regenerated by the body over a few weeks.
As always, your treating surgeon/physician will be able to further clarify your concerns.
